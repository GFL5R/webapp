# Technique Analogue Review

# Combat

## Acrobatic Recovery
**GFL5R:** When making a Combat skill check using Swiftness or Resilience, spend opportunities to remove Disoriented or Prone conditions from yourself, or prevent suffering them.
**L5R ([To Float or Sink](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/To_Float_or_Sink.html), Ninjutsu):** When making a Martial (Water or Earth) check, spend Water or Earth opportunities to remove an existing Disoriented or Prone condition from yourself or prevent suffering them if the check would cause it.

## Aggressive Escort
**GFL5R:** After a character attacks you or your ward while you're guarding them, perform a Strike action against that character with one readied weapon.
**L5R ([Crescent Moon Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crescent_Moon_Style.html), Kata):** When performing a Guard action, spend opportunities to allow a Strike action with one readied Martial Arts [Melee or Unarmed] weapon against a character at range 1–2 who attacks you or another character you are guarding, persisting until the start of your next turn or until you perform a Strike action this way.

## Airborne Assault
**GFL5R:** While performing a Strike action one or more range bands above your target, spend Swiftness opportunities to afflict chosen targets with the Disoriented condition.
**L5R ([Owl’s Swoop Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Owl’s_Swoop_Style.html), Kata):** While performing a Martial Arts (Melee) or Unarmed check from 1 or more range bands above your target horizontally, spend Air (op) to cause 1 target per opportunity spent to suffer the Disoriented condition.

## Anchor Point
**GFL5R:** When performing a Guard or Reinforce action, spend Resilience opportunities to force characters with low Focus to take strife to attack anyone but you, or cause enemy leaders to receive panic.
**L5R ([Great Anvil's Measure](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Great_Anvil's_Measure.html), Shuji):** When you make a check to perform a Guard action in a skirmish or a Reinforce action in a mass battle, spend Earth opportunities to force enemy characters or leaders to suffer strife or panic respectively when they attempt to attack or scheme against anyone other than you, with the effect persisting until the end of your next turn.

## Battle in the Mind
**GFL5R:** During a duel's Initiative check using Fortune, name two approaches your opponent cannot select as their posture on their first turn and learn one of their techniques.
**L5R ([Battle in the Mind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Battle_in_the_Mind.html), Kata):** When you make an Initiative check for a duel using your Void Ring, spend Void opportunities to force your opponent to name one of two rings you specify, preventing them from selecting that ring as their stance on their first turn, or spend Void+ opportunities to learn one of their known techniques of a chosen category per opportunity spent.

## Cleaving Strike
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with a melee weapon, spend two opportunities to replace a critical strike with a severity 5-6 Debilitating Gash that requires no resistance check.
**L5R ([Deadly Sting](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Deadly_Sting.html), Ninjutsu):** As an Attack action, spend one dose of poison and make a TN 3 Martial Arts (Air) check using a readied Concealable weapon to apply the poison to your weapon and deal physical damage equal to your weapon's deadliness plus bonus successes, with Air (op) allowing the target to suffer a critical strike of severity 2 plus 1 per opportunity spent.

## Closing Strike
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Blades (Swiftness) check to move into melee range and deal damage equal to your Swiftness value, with bonus damage if the target fails to resist.
**L5R ([Flowing Water Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Flowing_Water_Strike.html), Kata):** As an Attack and Movement action using a readied weapon, make a TN 3 Martial Arts (Water) check targeting one character at range 0–2 to inflict physical damage equal to your Water Ring and the Bleeding condition, forcing a TN 4 Fitness (Earth 2, Fire 5) resistance check where failure increases damage by the weapon's base damage, and spend Water opportunities to remove Dazed, Disoriented, Immobilized, or Prone conditions from yourself.

## Counterattack
**GFL5R:** When targeted by an Attack action at range 0, spend 1 Fortune point to redirect that attack to another character at range 0-1 if your Hand-to-Hand (Resilience) check succeeds.
**L5R ([Laughing Fox Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Laughing_Fox_Style.html), Kata):** When you are targeted by an Attack action with an empty hand, spend 1 Void point to make a Martial Arts (Unarmed) (Air) check targeting a character at range 0–1 other than the attacker with a TN equal to their vigilance; if you succeed, you cease being the target and the new target becomes the target of the action, and you may spend additional opportunities to deal Skulduggery damage, impose the Prone condition on both the attacker and the new target, or move 1 range band away from the attacker.

## Crushing Blow
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with a melee-type Exotic weapon, spend two Power opportunities to give one of the target's defensive items the Damaged quality, or Destroyed if already Damaged.
**L5R ([Scouring Wind Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Scouring_Wind_Style.html), Kata):** When you make an Attack action check using Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed] with a non-Razor-Edged weapon in two hands, spend (op)(op) to damage or destroy a target's defensive item instead of dealing damage, or spend (op)(op) with an ax to deal damage and damage or destroy a defensive item.

## Deflective Defense
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 4 Blades (Resilience) check to reduce incoming physical damage from ranged attacks by your Conditioning ranks plus bonus successes, optionally inflicting 2 strife on the attacker.
**L5R ([Deflective Defense](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Deflective_Defense.html), Kata):** As a Movement and Support action using a readied Cumbersome weapon, make a TN 2 Martial Arts [Melee] (Earth) check to increase your physical resistance against attacks from range 2–6 by your Fitness ranks, and spend Earth (op)(op) to inflict 2 strife on the attacker after defending against their physical damage.

## Disarming Shot
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 4 Firearms (Precision) check to deal damage and force a target to resist or lose control of a readied weapon, sending it 2 range bands away.
**L5R ([Crimson Leaves Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crimson_Leaves_Strike.html), Kata):** As an Attack action using a readied weapon, make a TN 4 Martial Arts (Earth) check against one character within range to deal physical damage equal to your Earth Ring plus bonus successes and force them to resist with a TN 4 Fitness check (Air 2, Water 5) or lose control of a chosen readied weapon, which travels 3 range bands in a direction of your choice, or take control of their weapon if you spend an Earth opportunity.

## Distraction Tactics
**GFL5R:** Make a Tactics (Resilience) check to cause characters to select you as their target instead of your allies for a number of rounds equal to your Fortification Value.
**L5R ([Feigned Opening](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Feigned_Opening.html), Shuji):** As a Movement and Scheme action during skirmish or mass battle, make a TN equal to the target's vigilance Performance (Air) check to convince one observing character that you are weaker or stronger than you actually are, allowing them to adjust their actions accordingly, with additional opportunities spent to target more characters or increase the TN of Attack checks against your cohort in mass battle.

## Eyes on the Prize
**GFL5R:** When performing a Strike action, spend Precision opportunities to learn one of the following about your target: their approach values, their conditions, or one technique they possess.
**L5R ([Clouds Parted by Steel](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Clouds_Parted_by_Steel.html), Shuji):** When you make a check to perform a Strike or Assault action, spend Air (op) to learn one of your target's advantages or disadvantages if their vigilance is lower than your ranks in Sentiment plus * spent this way.

## Eyes Up!
**GFL5R:** Once per session, make a TN 2 Resolve (Swiftness) check to reduce a target's Focus by 2 and increase their Vigilance by 2 until end of scene, with options to target more characters or extend the duration.
**L5R ([Eyes Up!](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Eyes_Up!.html), Shuji):** As a Support action or once per session at a scene's start, make a TN 2 Scholar (Water) check targeting one character to reduce their focus by 2 and increase their vigilance by 2 until the end of the scene, with additional targets per Water opportunity spent or extended to the next scene with one opportunity.

## Feigned Opening
**GFL5R:** Make a Performance (Swiftness) check to convince a target you are weaker or stronger than you are, optionally affecting additional targets or increasing the TN of attacks against your cohort.
**L5R ([Feigned Opening](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Feigned_Opening.html), Shuji):** As a Movement and Scheme action during skirmish or mass battle, make a TN equal to the target's vigilance Performance (Air) check to convince one observing character that you are weaker or stronger than you actually are, allowing them to adjust their actions accordingly, with additional opportunities spent to target more characters or increase the TN of Attack checks against your cohort in mass battle.

## Gap in the Armor
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with a bladed weapon, spend opportunities to make your weapon ignore that much armor on the target.
**L5R ([Veiled Menace Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Veiled_Menace_Style.html), Kata):** When you make a Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed] Attack action check using a one-handed weapon, spend (op) to select an unaware or Disoriented target and inflict a critical strike with severity equal to your weapon's deadliness plus 1 per (op) spent beyond the first.

## Grappler
**GFL5R:** Make a Hand-to-Hand (Fortune) check with TN equal to the target's silhouette to immobilize them and grant your allies assistance on attacks against them until the start of your next turn.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Guns Akimbo
**GFL5R:** When making a Firearms Attack action with a second readied weapon, spend opportunities equal to the target's Vigilance to deal additional damage with the second weapon, increased if the target is Dazed.
**L5R ([Spinning Blades Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Spinning_Blades_Style.html), Kata):** When making a Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed] Attack action check with a second readied weapon, spend (op) equal to your target’s Vigilance to use that weapon against one target, inflicting physical damage equal to its base damage, or if the target is Dazed, increase this damage by your ranks in the skill used for the check.

## Hand of God
**GFL5R:** Make a Firearms (Precision) check at range greater than 3, with TN equal to the weapon's normal TN plus the target's Vigilance, to deal double the weapon's base damage plus bonus successes.
**L5R ([Silencing Stroke](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Silencing_Stroke.html), Ninjutsu):** As an Attack action, make a TN 4 Martial Arts (Air) check using a readied weapon against one character within range to deal physical damage and impose the Silenced condition, while characters beyond range 0–2 do not notice the strike for 1 round plus bonus rounds equal to bonus successes, and spend Air opportunities to increase critical strike severity.

## Improvise Weapon
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 2 Survival (Swiftness) check to find and ready an improvised weapon with damage equal to your Survival ranks, optionally performing an immediate Strike or granting it a special quality.
**L5R ([Improvised Assault](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Improvised_Assault.html), Kata):** As a Movement and Support action, make a TN 2 Tactics (Water) or Smithing (Water) check using a readied improvised weapon to treat it as a Martial Arts [Melee] weapon with reduced damage and deadliness until the scene ends, gaining the Destroyed quality afterward, and spend Water opportunities to immediately Strike or grant the weapon Cumbersome, Razor-Edged, or Snaring qualities.

## Judo Throw
**GFL5R:** When making a Blades or Hand-to-Hand attack at range 0, spend Power opportunities to force the target to resist or become Prone and take fatigue equal to their resistance shortfall.
**L5R ([Iron in the Mountains Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Iron_in_the_Mountains_Style.html), Kata):** When you make a Martial Arts (Earth) check, spend Earth opportunities to force a target to resist with a TN 3 Fitness (Air 1, Water 4) check or suffer the Prone condition and fatigue equal to their shortfall, increasing the resistance TN by 1 per opportunity spent.

## Landmine
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Explosives (Precision) check to place a concealed mine at range 0-1 that detonates when a character approaches, dealing damage and applying conditions based on scene type.
**L5R ([Employ Terrible Thunder](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Employ_Terrible_Thunder.html), Ninjutsu):** As a Movement and Support action, spend 1 charge of blasting powder and forfeit half your honor rank (rounded up) to make a TN 4 Skulduggery check and place the powder at range 0–1, which detonates at the end of the next round to inflict damage, conditions, and terrain effects based on the scene type, with opportunities to upgrade damage to destruction, create dangerous terrain, or delay detonation.

## Last Stand
**GFL5R:** When resisting a critical strike while Enraged in a skirmish, spend Power opportunities to deal damage equal to your unarmed or improvised weapon to all nearby characters with Vigilance lower than the opportunities spent.
**L5R ([Boar’s Wrath Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Boar's_Wrath_Style.html), Kata):** When you make a check to resist a critical strike during a skirmish while Enraged and not Incapacitated, spend Fire or Void opportunities to deal physical damage to nearby characters equal to your unarmed profile's base damage plus 1 for each opportunity spent beyond their vigilance.

## Let It Ride
**GFL5R:** After performing an action without a check while in a Fortune Posture, make a TN 1 Resolve check to reduce strife you receive by 3 plus 1 per two bonus successes.
**L5R ([Mercy of the Stone Fortune](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kiho/Mercy_of_the_Stone_Fortune.html), Kiho):** As a Support action, make a TN 1 Meditation (Earth) check to activate a kihō that allows you to absorb up to your Earth ring in fatigue to reduce damage for a friendly character at range 0–1, or spend 1 Void point to take a critical strike in their place, with bonus successes increasing your Fitness ranks by 2 for rounds equal to those successes.

## Momentous Strike
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with a bladed weapon, spend a Swiftness opportunity to increase the weapon's deadliness by the number of range bands you traveled this round.
**L5R ([Piercing Bolt Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Piercing_Bolt_Style.html), Kata):** When making an Attack action check with an ax or polearm, spend opportunities to treat your weapon's deadliness as increased by the number of range bands you have traveled this round, and if you threw it, add the number of range bands it has traveled.

## Murderous Intent
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 4 Combat (Power) check to inflict a critical strike with severity equal to weapon deadliness plus bonus successes and a reduced TN to resist, moving 1 range band toward the target if you fail.
**L5R ([Heartpiercing Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Heartpiercing_Strike.html), Kata):** As an Attack and Movement action using a readied weapon, make a TN 4 Martial Arts (Fire) check targeting one character within range; if you succeed, your target suffers a critical strike with severity equal to your weapon's deadliness plus bonus successes at a TN of 3 (Air 4, Water 1) to resist, while if you fail you suffer Disoriented, and you may spend Fire opportunities to move up to 1 range band toward your target before resolving the effect.

## Pistol Whip
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Conditioning (Resilience) check targeting one character at range 0 to deal physical damage equal to 5 plus bonus successes with deadliness 2, optionally pushing them 1 range band away.
**L5R ([Thunderclap Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Thunderclap_Strike.html), Kata):** As an Attack action using a readied weapon, make a TN 3 Martial Arts (Air) check targeting each character within range to deal base damage and force them to resist with a TN 3 Fitness check or move 2 range bands away, with Air opportunities increasing the resistance TN by 1 per opportunity spent.

## Rolling With the Punches
**GFL5R:** When making a Conditioning check to resist a critical strike, spend Fortune opportunities to reduce the critical strike's severity by an additional point per two opportunities spent.
**L5R ([Resilient Readiness](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-mastery/Resilient_Readiness.html), Mastery):** When making a check to investigate a supernatural phenomenon, spend opportunities to increase your resistance against that specific phenomenon by an amount equal to the opportunities spent, with the effect persisting until the end of the scene.

## Shoot to Maim
**GFL5R:** While performing an Attack action, spend two Precision opportunities to choose a condition; if this attack inflicts that condition, the target cannot remove it until the end of your next turn.
**L5R ([Bear’s Swipe Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Bear’s_Swipe_Style.html), Kata):** While performing an Attack action check, spend Earth (op)(op) to choose one of Bleeding, Dazed, Disoriented, or Prone, preventing targets from removing that condition until the end of your next turn if the check inflicts it.

## Sighting-in
**GFL5R:** When making a ranged attack with Swiftness, spend two Swiftness opportunities to reduce the TN penalty for attacking outside ideal range by 1 until you move or the scene ends.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Sniper's Advantage
**GFL5R:** Once per scene, make a Firearms (Fortune) Attack and Movement action with a long-range weapon at range 6+; on success, the target suffers a critical strike at the start of their next turn.
**L5R ([Falling Heavens Shot](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Falling_Heavens_Shot.html), Kata):** As an Attack and Movement action using a readied ranged weapon, make a Martial Arts [Ranged] (Void) check targeting one character within range with a TN equal to their vigilance; if successful, they suffer a critical strike with severity equal to your weapon's deadliness at the start of their next turn, and you may delay this effect for one additional round per opportunity spent, increasing the deadliness by 2 for each round delayed.

## Splat!
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with a melee-type Exotic weapon, if you fail the target suffers damage equal to your Conditioning ranks; if you succeed and the target is Prone, increase damage by your Fitness ranks.
**L5R ([Rushing Avalanche Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Rushing_Avalanche_Style.html), Kata):** When making a Martial Arts (Melee) check with a blunt weapon, spend opportunities to deal physical damage equal to your Fitness ranks on a failure with a shortfall of two or less, or increase damage by your Fitness ranks if you succeed and the target is Prone.

## Throwing Knife
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 2 Blades check to hurl a knife at a target at range 1, dealing the knife's base damage plus bonus successes, optionally reducing the TN of the next attack against that target by 1.
**L5R ([Soaring Slice](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Soaring_Slice.html), Kata):** As an Attack action using one readied weapon in a one-handed grip, make a TN 2 Martial Arts check targeting one character at range 2–3 to hurl the weapon at the target, dealing physical damage equal to the weapon's base damage plus bonus successes if successful, or sending it a number of range bands equal to the technique's maximum range if failed, with (op) spent to increase maximum range by 1 per opportunity and (op) to reduce the TN of the next Attack action against the target by 1.

## Whirling Strike
**GFL5R:** When making an Attack action with an Exotic weapon, deal additional damage equal to weapon deadliness to minion targets on success, or deal strife equal to weapon deadliness to low-Resolve targets on failure.
**L5R ([Swirling Tempest Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Swirling_Tempest_Style.html), Kata):** When making a Martial Arts [Melee] check with a blunt weapon or polearm, spend (op) to deal physical damage equal to your weapon's base damage to one character in range with vigilance lower than or equal to (op) spent, and you may activate this (op) multiple times to target different characters.

## Wrest Control
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 4 Hand-to-Hand (Power) check at range 0 to deal damage and force the target to resist or surrender control of one readied weapon to you.
**L5R ([Crimson Leaves Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crimson_Leaves_Strike.html), Kata):** As an Attack action using a readied weapon, make a TN 4 Martial Arts (Earth) check against one character within range to deal physical damage equal to your Earth Ring plus bonus successes and force them to resist with a TN 4 Fitness check (Air 2, Water 5) or lose control of a chosen readied weapon, which travels 3 range bands in a direction of your choice, or take control of their weapon if you spend an Earth opportunity.

## Breach and Clear
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Tactics (Swiftness) check while wielding a shield to move into range 0 of a target at range 1-3, daze them on a failed resistance check, and add your shield's Defensive quality to your resistance until the start of your next turn.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Bulwark
**GFL5R:** Once per scene, make a TN 4 Tactics (Resilience) check while wielding a shield to protect characters at range 0-1, adding your shield's Defensive quality to their resistance and forcing attacks targeting them to target you instead.
**L5R ([Power of the Earth Dragon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Power_of_the_Earth_Dragon.html), Invocations):** As a Support action, make a TN 4 Theology (Earth) check targeting one character at range 0–2 to augment their endurance by your Earth Ring plus bonus successes for one round plus additional rounds equal to bonus successes, with Earth opportunities extending the effect to the end of the scene.

## Intercept
**GFL5R:** When an Attack action targets a character at range 0 of you, spend 1 Fortune Point to become the target instead, adding your shield's Defensive quality to your resistance if wielding one.
**L5R ([Vigilance of Mind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-school/Vigilance_of_Mind.html), School):** Once per round, when another character within range of one of your readied melee weapons would suffer physical damage, spend fatigue up to your school rank to reduce the damage the target suffers by twice that amount.

## Shield Bash
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Hand-to-Hand (Power) check while wielding a shield to deal damage equal to the shield's base damage plus bonus successes, dazing and pushing the target on a failed resistance check.
**L5R ([Akilah's Teachings](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-school/Akilah's_Teachings.html), School):** Once per round after an Attack action check against you fails, if you have a shield readied and the attack is at range 0–1, you may shield bash, dealing physical damage equal to your ranks in Fitness plus your school rank.

# Command

## Flexible Positioning
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Command (Swiftness) check to allow up to your Command rank in allies to switch postures and move 1 range band immediately.
**L5R ([Hands of the Tides](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Hands_of_the_Tides.html), Invocations):** As a Movement action, make a TN 5 Theology (Water) check targeting yourself and up to your Water Ring in range 1–4 to exchange the positions of the summoned targets, causing each to receive 2 strife, and spend Water opportunities to assign a new stance to one target per opportunity spent.

## No Retreat
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 5 Command (Fortune) check to prevent morale collapse in Mass Battle or allow allies to ignore high Strife conditions for one round plus bonus rounds.
**L5R ([Battle of No Escape](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Battle_of_No_Escape.html), Shuji):** As a Support action during a mass battle, intrigue, duel, or skirmish, make a TN 5 Command (Void) check (TN 2 if all targets are in Confining or Entangling terrain) to prevent your army from suffering morale collapse when panic exceeds discipline or to have targets ignore the Compromised condition, with Void opportunities allowing additional targets or ignoring the Incapacitated condition.

## Plant a Seed
**GFL5R:** Make a Tactics (Precision) check against the enemy commander's Vigilance to change your army's strategic goal and apply momentum to the new objective.
**L5R ([Entice with Falsehoods](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Entice_with_Falsehoods.html), Shuji):** As a Scheme action in Mass Battle targeting an enemy commander, make a TN equal to their vigilance Tactics (Air) check (stake 5 honor and 5 glory if not your commander, reduce TN by 2 if in Obscuring Terrain) to immediately change your army's strategic goal and apply accrued momentum to it, with Air opportunities spent to keep targets unaware of your machinations.

## Sacrifice Play
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 5 Command (Fortune) check to allow targets to ignore critical strikes below your Fortune value until scene end, or during Mass Battle remove panic from your army each round.
**L5R ([A Samurai’s Fate](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/A_Samurai’s_Fate.html), Shuji):** As an action, make a TN 5 Command (Void) check targeting any number of characters (or your cohort in a mass battle) to grant them immunity to critical strikes with severity lower than your Void Ring plus bonus successes until the end of the scene, or to grant your army the Fearless Army ability that removes panic equal to your Glory rank at the end of each round.

## Scorched Earth
**GFL5R:** Make a Subterfuge (Swiftness) check to sabotage an enemy unit's fortification, reduce its defense, or remove a special ability, forcing the target commander to resist or suffer morale disruption.
**L5R ([Wreak Havoc](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Wreak_Havoc.html), Ninjutsu):** As an Attack and Movement action during a mass battle, spend 1 charge of blasting powder and forfeit honor equal to half your honor rank (rounded up) to reduce the TN by 2 (to a minimum of 1) when making a Skulduggery (Fire) check targeting one enemy leader's cohort with a TN equal to that leader's vigilance, and if successful, reduce a fortification's difficulty by 4 plus bonus successes (destroying it if 0 or lower and causing attrition and critical strikes), remove Confining and Entangling terrain while adding Dangerous and Obscuring, remove one cohort ability, force the target leader to resist a TN 5 Command check or suffer panic, and optionally spend additional charges to add bonus successes.

## Shock and Awe
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 3 Command (Swiftness) check to increase allies' initiative in the next round of a skirmish, or inflict panic on an enemy leader's cohort in Mass Battle.
**L5R ([Descending Swarm Shot](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Descending_Swarm_Shot.html), Kata):** As an Attack action using a readied bow or sling, make a TN 3 Martial Arts [Ranged] (Air) check against a target within range (reducing TN by 1 for silhouette 3 or larger) to make them vulnerable, granting bonus successes to allies who successfully attack them until the end of your next turn, or extend the duration by one round per (op)(op) spent.

# Electronic Warfare

## Blackout
**GFL5R:** Disable all devices on a target node for a number of rounds equal to bonus successes, with an option to shield one device from the effect.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Cleanup Detail
**GFL5R:** Remove all traces of your presence on a target node where you hold a foothold, preventing detection of who accessed it or what was done.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Dead Drop
**GFL5R:** Plant a hidden data cache in a node that stays concealed until end of scene or discovery, allowing you or allies to retrieve its contents as a free action.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Exfiltrate
**GFL5R:** Extract a named dataset from a node where you hold a foothold to your deck or storage device.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Falsify Data
**GFL5R:** Replace a target file or record with a convincing forgery detectable only with a TN 2 Computers check, with options to increase that detection difficulty.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Hijack
**GFL5R:** Take control of a networked device on a node where you hold a foothold and issue commands to it while maintaining concentration.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## ICE Breaker
**GFL5R:** Deal damage equal to your EW Rating plus Power plus bonus successes to a sentry, ignoring the first 2 points of its Integrity for fragging purposes.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Neural Fortification
**GFL5R:** Increase a T-Doll's Security Rating by 1 and negate Security Rating reduction from Level II until end of scene.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Overclock
**GFL5R:** Increase your EW Rating by 1 until the end of your next turn, after which your deck suffers the Damaged quality.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Puppet String
**GFL5R:** Force a target T-Doll to perform one Movement action of your choice on their next turn, with an option to inflict 3 strife from the violation.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Rootkit
**GFL5R:** Install a rootkit on a node where you hold a foothold, allowing you to target objects on the network with techniques that require a foothold regardless of your position, lasting rounds equal to bonus successes.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Siege Engine
**GFL5R:** Reduce the network's Security Rating by 1 for the remainder of the scene, affecting check TNs for all characters, with an option to reduce by 2 instead.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Spoof
**GFL5R:** Make a sensor, camera, or input device report false data for 1 plus bonus successes rounds, detectable with a TN 2 Computers check.
**L5R:** *(no analogue)*

## Strike
**GFL5R:** Deal damage equal to your EW Rating plus bonus successes to a character or sentry on your current node, with an option to inflict a critical strike with severity equal to EW Rating plus 2.

## Silent Strike
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 4 Precision Attack check to deal weapon damage, inflict the Silenced condition, and prevent characters at range 3+ from noticing the strike for one round plus bonus successes.

## Vanishing Act
**GFL5R:** Make a TN 5 Survival (Swiftness) check to conceal yourself and up to your Tactics ranks of allies in Entangling or Obscuring terrain, preventing detection and attacks until end of your next turn or until an Attack action is taken.
**L5R ([Vanish in the Shadows](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Vanish_in_the_Shadows.html), Ninjutsu):** As a downtime activity or once per scene as a Movement and Support action, make a TN 5 Survival (Air) check targeting your cohort or allies in Confining, Entangling, or Obscuring terrain (using Skulduggery or Seafaring in urban or ocean environments) to hide them from characters with vigilance lower than or equal to your Air Ring plus bonus successes, preventing them from being targeted or performing Attack actions until the end of your next turn or until they attack, and spend opportunities to inflict panic on enemy cohorts or strife on nearby characters, or increase the TN of Attack checks against your targets if you fail.

## Volatile Dispersal
**GFL5R:** Expend two substance doses and make a TN 2 Science (Swiftness) check to deal 3 damage to all characters at range 0-2 and force them to resist the substance's effects.
**L5R ([Noxious Cloud](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Noxious_Cloud.html), Ninjutsu):** As an Attack action, spend two doses of any single poison and make a TN 2 Medicine (Air) check targeting each character at range 0–2 to inflict 3 physical damage ignoring resistance, with Air (op) allowing movement, Air (op)+ creating Obscuring and Dangerous terrain, and Air (op)+ enabling critical strikes on one target per opportunity spent.
