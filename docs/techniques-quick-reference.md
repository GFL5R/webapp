# Techniques Quick Reference

# Combat

## Acrobatic Recovery
When making a Combat skill check using Swiftness or Resilience, spend opportunities to remove Disoriented or Prone conditions from yourself, or prevent suffering them.
*L5R analogue: [To Float or Sink](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/To_Float_or_Sink.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Aggressive Escort
After a character attacks you or your ward while you're guarding them, perform a Strike action against that character with one readied weapon.
*L5R analogue: [Crescent Moon Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crescent_Moon_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Airborne Assault
While performing a Strike action one or more range bands above your target, spend Swiftness opportunities to afflict chosen targets with the Disoriented condition.
*L5R analogue: [Owl’s Swoop Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Owl’s_Swoop_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Anchor Point
When performing a Guard or Reinforce action, spend Resilience opportunities to force characters with low Focus to take strife to attack anyone but you, or cause enemy leaders to receive panic.
*L5R analogue: [Great Anvil's Measure](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Great_Anvil's_Measure.html) (Shuji)*

## Battle in the Mind
During a duel's Initiative check using Fortune, name two approaches your opponent cannot select as their posture on their first turn and learn one of their techniques.
*L5R analogue: [Battle in the Mind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Battle_in_the_Mind.html) (Kata)*

## Cleaving Strike
When making an Attack action with a melee weapon, spend two opportunities to replace a critical strike with a severity 5-6 Debilitating Gash that requires no resistance check.
*L5R analogue: [Deadly Sting](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Deadly_Sting.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Closing Strike
Make a TN 3 Blades (Swiftness) check to move into melee range and deal damage equal to your Swiftness value, with bonus damage if the target fails to resist.
*L5R analogue: [Flowing Water Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Flowing_Water_Strike.html) (Kata)*

## Counterattack
When targeted by an Attack action at range 0, spend 1 Fortune point to redirect that attack to another character at range 0-1 if your Hand-to-Hand (Resilience) check succeeds.
*L5R analogue: [Laughing Fox Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Laughing_Fox_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Crushing Blow
When making an Attack action with a melee-type Exotic weapon, spend two Power opportunities to give one of the target's defensive items the Damaged quality, or Destroyed if already Damaged.
*L5R analogue: [Scouring Wind Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Scouring_Wind_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Deflective Defense
Make a TN 4 Blades (Resilience) check to reduce incoming physical damage from ranged attacks by your Conditioning ranks plus bonus successes, optionally inflicting 2 strife on the attacker.
*L5R analogue: [Deflective Defense](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Deflective_Defense.html) (Kata)*

## Disarming Shot
Make a TN 4 Firearms (Precision) check to deal damage and force a target to resist or lose control of a readied weapon, sending it 2 range bands away.
*L5R analogue: [Crimson Leaves Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crimson_Leaves_Strike.html) (Kata)*

## Distraction Tactics
Make a Tactics (Resilience) check to cause characters to select you as their target instead of your allies for a number of rounds equal to your Fortification Value.
*L5R analogue: [Lord Shiba's Valor (Phoenix)](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Lord_Shiba's_Valor_(Phoenix).html) (Kata)*

## Eyes on the Prize
When performing a Strike action, spend Precision opportunities to learn one of the following about your target: their approach values, their conditions, or one technique they possess.
*L5R analogue: [Clouds Parted by Steel](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Clouds_Parted_by_Steel.html) (Shuji)*

## Eyes Up!
Once per session, make a TN 2 Resolve (Swiftness) check to reduce a target's Focus by 2 and increase their Vigilance by 2 until end of scene, with options to target more characters or extend the duration.
*L5R analogue: [Eyes Up!](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Eyes_Up!.html) (Shuji)*

## Feigned Opening
Make a Performance (Swiftness) check to convince a target you are weaker or stronger than you are, optionally affecting additional targets or increasing the TN of attacks against your cohort.
*L5R analogue: [Feigned Opening](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Feigned_Opening.html) (Shuji)*

## Gap in the Armor
When making an Attack action with a bladed weapon, spend opportunities to make your weapon ignore that much armor on the target.

## Grappler
Make a Hand-to-Hand (Fortune) check with TN equal to the target's silhouette to immobilize them and grant your allies assistance on attacks against them until the start of your next turn.
*L5R analogue: [Lord Hida’s Grip (Crab)](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Lord_Hida’s_Grip_(Crab).html) (Kata)*

## Guns Akimbo
When making a Firearms Attack action with a second readied weapon, spend opportunities equal to the target's Vigilance to deal additional damage with the second weapon, increased if the target is Dazed.
*L5R analogue: [Spinning Blades Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Spinning_Blades_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Hand of God
Make a Firearms (Precision) check at range greater than 3, with TN equal to the weapon's normal TN plus the target's Vigilance, to deal double the weapon's base damage plus bonus successes.

## Improvise Weapon
Make a TN 2 Survival (Swiftness) check to find and ready an improvised weapon with damage equal to your Survival ranks, optionally performing an immediate Strike or granting it a special quality.
*L5R analogue: [Improvised Assault](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Improvised_Assault.html) (Kata)*

## Judo Throw
When making a Blades or Hand-to-Hand attack at range 0, spend Power opportunities to force the target to resist or become Prone and take fatigue equal to their resistance shortfall.
*L5R analogue: [Iron in the Mountains Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Iron_in_the_Mountains_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Landmine
Make a TN 3 Explosives (Precision) check to place a concealed mine at range 0-1 that detonates when a character approaches, dealing damage and applying conditions based on scene type.
*L5R analogue: [Employ Terrible Thunder](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Employ_Terrible_Thunder.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Last Stand
When resisting a critical strike while Enraged in a skirmish, spend Power opportunities to deal damage equal to your unarmed or improvised weapon to all nearby characters with Vigilance lower than the opportunities spent.
*L5R analogue: [Boar’s Wrath Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Boar's_Wrath_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Let It Ride
After performing an action without a check while in a Fortune Posture, make a TN 1 Resolve check to reduce strife you receive by 3 plus 1 per two bonus successes.
*L5R analogue: [Mind's Edge](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Mind's_Edge.html) (Kata)*

## Momentous Strike
When making an Attack action with a bladed weapon, spend a Swiftness opportunity to increase the weapon's deadliness by the number of range bands you traveled this round.
*L5R analogue: [Piercing Bolt Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Piercing_Bolt_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Murderous Intent
Make a TN 4 Combat (Power) check to inflict a critical strike with severity equal to weapon deadliness plus bonus successes and a reduced TN to resist, moving 1 range band toward the target if you fail.
*L5R analogue: [Heartpiercing Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Heartpiercing_Strike.html) (Kata)*

## Pistol Whip
Make a TN 3 Conditioning (Resilience) check targeting one character at range 0 to deal physical damage equal to 5 plus bonus successes with deadliness 2, optionally pushing them 1 range band away.
*L5R analogue: [Thunderclap Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Thunderclap_Strike.html) (Kata)*

## Rolling With the Punches
When making a Conditioning check to resist a critical strike, spend Fortune opportunities to reduce the critical strike's severity by an additional point per two opportunities spent.
*L5R analogue: [Void Embrace Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Void_Embrace_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Shoot to Maim
While performing an Attack action, spend two Precision opportunities to choose a condition; if this attack inflicts that condition, the target cannot remove it until the end of your next turn.
*L5R analogue: [Bear’s Swipe Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Bear’s_Swipe_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Sighting-in
When making a ranged attack with Swiftness, spend two Swiftness opportunities to reduce the TN penalty for attacking outside ideal range by 1 until you move or the scene ends.

## Sniper's Advantage
Once per scene, make a Firearms (Fortune) Attack and Movement action with a long-range weapon at range 6+; on success, the target suffers a critical strike at the start of their next turn.
*L5R analogue: [Falling Heavens Shot](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Falling_Heavens_Shot.html) (Kata)*

## Splat!
When making an Attack action with a melee-type Exotic weapon, if you fail the target suffers damage equal to your Conditioning ranks; if you succeed and the target is Prone, increase damage by your Fitness ranks.
*L5R analogue: [Rushing Avalanche Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Rushing_Avalanche_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Throwing Knife
Make a TN 2 Blades check to hurl a knife at a target at range 1, dealing the knife's base damage plus bonus successes, optionally reducing the TN of the next attack against that target by 1.
*L5R analogue: [Soaring Slice](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Soaring_Slice.html) (Kata)*

## Whirling Strike
When making an Attack action with an Exotic weapon, deal additional damage equal to weapon deadliness to minion targets on success, or deal strife equal to weapon deadliness to low-Resolve targets on failure.
*L5R analogue: [Swirling Tempest Style](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Swirling_Tempest_Style.html) (Kata)*

## Wrest Control
Make a TN 4 Hand-to-Hand (Power) check at range 0 to deal damage and force the target to resist or surrender control of one readied weapon to you.
*L5R analogue: [Crimson Leaves Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Crimson_Leaves_Strike.html) (Kata)*

## Breach and Clear
Make a TN 3 Tactics (Swiftness) check while wielding a shield to move into range 0 of a target at range 1-3, daze them on a failed resistance check, and add your shield's Defensive quality to your resistance until the start of your next turn.
*L5R analogue: [Lord Akodo’s Roar](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lord_Akodo’s_Roar_(Lion).html) (Shuji)*

## Bulwark
Once per scene, make a TN 4 Tactics (Resilience) check while wielding a shield to protect characters at range 0-1, adding your shield's Defensive quality to their resistance and forcing attacks targeting them to target you instead.
*L5R analogue: [Power of the Earth Dragon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Power_of_the_Earth_Dragon.html) (Invocations)*

## Intercept
When an Attack action targets a character at range 0 of you, spend 1 Fortune Point to become the target instead, adding your shield's Defensive quality to your resistance if wielding one.
*L5R analogue: [Vigilance of Mind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-school/Vigilance_of_Mind.html) (School)*

## Shield Bash
Make a TN 3 Hand-to-Hand (Power) check while wielding a shield to deal damage equal to the shield's base damage plus bonus successes, dazing and pushing the target on a failed resistance check.

# Command

## Flexible Positioning
Make a TN 3 Command (Swiftness) check to allow up to your Command rank in allies to switch postures and move 1 range band immediately.
*L5R analogue: [Hands of the Tides](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Hands_of_the_Tides.html) (Invocations)*

## No Retreat
Make a TN 5 Command (Fortune) check to prevent morale collapse in Mass Battle or allow allies to ignore high Strife conditions for one round plus bonus rounds.
*L5R analogue: [Battle of No Escape](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Battle_of_No_Escape.html) (Shuji)*

## Plant a Seed
Make a Tactics (Precision) check against the enemy commander's Vigilance to change your army's strategic goal and apply momentum to the new objective.
*L5R analogue: [Entice with Falsehoods](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Entice_with_Falsehoods.html) (Shuji)*

## Sacrifice Play
Make a TN 5 Command (Fortune) check to allow targets to ignore critical strikes below your Fortune value until scene end, or during Mass Battle remove panic from your army each round.
*L5R analogue: [A Samurai’s Fate](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/A_Samurai’s_Fate.html) (Shuji)*

## Scorched Earth
Make a Subterfuge (Swiftness) check to sabotage an enemy unit's fortification, reduce its defense, or remove a special ability, forcing the target commander to resist or suffer morale disruption.
*L5R analogue: [Wreak Havoc](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Wreak_Havoc.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Shock and Awe
Make a TN 3 Command (Swiftness) check to increase allies' initiative in the next round of a skirmish, or inflict panic on an enemy leader's cohort in Mass Battle.
*L5R analogue: [Descending Swarm Shot](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Descending_Swarm_Shot.html) (Kata)*

# Electronic Warfare

## Blackout
Disable all devices on a target node for a number of rounds equal to bonus successes, with an option to shield one device from the effect.

## Cleanup Detail
Remove all traces of your presence on a target node where you hold a foothold, preventing detection of who accessed it or what was done.

## Dead Drop
Plant a hidden data cache in a node that stays concealed until end of scene or discovery, allowing you or allies to retrieve its contents as a free action.

## Exfiltrate
Extract a named dataset from a node where you hold a foothold to your deck or storage device.

## Falsify Data
Replace a target file or record with a convincing forgery detectable only with a TN 2 Computers check, with options to increase that detection difficulty.

## Hijack
Take control of a networked device on a node where you hold a foothold and issue commands to it while maintaining concentration.

## ICE Breaker
Deal damage equal to your EW Rating plus Power plus bonus successes to a sentry, ignoring the first 2 points of its Integrity for fragging purposes.

## Neural Fortification
Increase a T-Doll's Security Rating by 1 and negate Security Rating reduction from Level II until end of scene.

## Overclock
Increase your EW Rating by 1 until the end of your next turn, after which your deck suffers the Damaged quality.

## Puppet String
Force a target T-Doll to perform one Movement action of your choice on their next turn, with an option to inflict 3 strife from the violation.

## Rootkit
Install a rootkit on a node where you hold a foothold, allowing you to target objects on the network with techniques that require a foothold regardless of your position, lasting rounds equal to bonus successes.

## Siege Engine
Reduce the network's Security Rating by 1 for the remainder of the scene, affecting check TNs for all characters, with an option to reduce by 2 instead.

## Spoof
Make a sensor, camera, or input device report false data for 1 plus bonus successes rounds, detectable with a TN 2 Computers check.

## Strike
Deal damage equal to your EW Rating plus bonus successes to a character or sentry on your current node, with an option to inflict a critical strike with severity equal to EW Rating plus 2.

## Total Eclipse
Assume administrative control of the entire network for a number of rounds equal to bonus successes, after which the network crashes and you go to Heat 10 (Burned).

# Science

## Ballistic Analysis
Make a TN 2 Science (Precision) check to determine the approximate origin point of an attack by analyzing ballistic evidence.

## Chemical Analysis
Make a TN 2 Science (Resilience) check to identify a chemical sample's composition, properties, and likely origin sources.

## Chemical Synthesis
As a downtime activity, make a Science (Precision) check to manufacture a chemical substance if you have access to the necessary materials and equipment.

## Emergency Triage
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Medicine (Swiftness) check to identify all injured characters within range 0-1 and their general wound severity.

## Examine for ELID
Make a TN 2 Science (Precision) check to determine whether a subject is infected with ELID and, if so, its current progression stage.

## Forensic Analysis
Make a TN 2 Science (Precision) check to determine cause of death and the sequence of events that led to it.

## Hazard Detection
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Science (Fortune) check to detect radiation, biological agents, chemical weapons, or other environmental dangers within range 0-3.

## Jack of All Trades
Once per scene as a Support action, make a TN 4 Science (Fortune) check to treat your rating in one chosen technical skill as equal to your Science rating until end of scene, if it would otherwise be lower.

## Stabilize the Dying
As a Support action, make a TN 4 Medicine (Resilience) check to remove the dying condition from a dying character.

## Stop the Bleeding
As a Support action, make a TN 4 Medicine (Swiftness) check to stop active bleeding on an injured character.

# Social

## Appreciate the Scenery
When making a Support action check in Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring terrain, spend opportunities to remove strife or grant bonus dice.
*L5R analogue: [Appreciate the Scenery](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Appreciate_the_Scenery.html) (Shuji)*

## Artisan's Appraisal
When making an Arts or Performance (Precision) check to assess an object or performance, spend opportunities to learn the creator's approach ratings, advantages, disadvantages, or emotional state.
*L5R analogue: [Artisan’s Appraisal](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Artisan’s_Appraisal.html) (Shuji)*

## Bend with the Storm
When making a Social skill check using Precision, spend opportunities to make the target believe you possess a chosen Advantage or Disadvantage.
*L5R analogue: [Bend with the Storm](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Bend_with_the_Storm.html) (Shuji)*

## Beware the Smallest Mouse
When making a Social skill check using Swiftness, spend opportunities to reduce the target's Vigilance and lower the TN of their Social checks targeting you until end of scene.
*L5R analogue: [Beware The Smallest Mouse](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Beware_The_Smallest_Mouse.html) (Shuji)*

## Bond of Heroes
Once per session as a Support action, make a TN 5 Performance (Fortune) check targeting characters you share a meaningful bond with to remove Strife and Fatigue.
*L5R analogue: [Bond of Heroes](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Bond_of_Heroes.html) (Shuji)*

## Borrowed Courage
Make a TN 4 Performance (Precision) check during downtime to spread rumors among a target army or group of characters, reducing panic they receive in the next scene.
*L5R analogue: [Borrowed Courage](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Borrowed_Courage.html) (Shuji)*

## Bravado
Make a Performance or Arts (Power) Scheme action targeting any number of characters to make them perceive your Fame as 10 higher or lower than your actual value.
*L5R analogue: [Bravado](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Bravado.html) (Shuji)*

## Cadence
When making a Social skill check using Precision, spend opportunities to convey secret messages to characters who do or don't possess this technique.
*L5R analogue: [Cadence](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Cadence.html) (Shuji)*

## Commanding Authority
Once per session, make a Command (Fortune) check against the highest-Fame target's Vigilance to prevent up to your Command rank in targets from performing Attack actions targeting you.
*L5R analogue: [Lady Doji’s Decree](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lady_Doji’s_Decree_(Crane).html) (Shuji)*

## Commanding Presence
When entering a scene, make a Performance (Power) check against the gap between the highest Fame present and your own to reduce the TN of your Scheme and Support actions for one round.
*L5R analogue: [Glorious Entrance](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Glorious_Entrance.html) (Shuji)*

## Concession
Make a TN 3 Negotiation (Precision) Scheme action targeting a character, giving up something they want to prevent them from adding momentum to their social objectives.
*L5R analogue: [Entice with Offerings](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Entice_with_Offerings.html) (Shuji)*

## Dramatic Entrance
Make a Culture (Swiftness) Scheme action against the highest-Fame target's Vigilance to enter or exit a conversation without social penalty.
*L5R analogue: [Buoyant Arrival](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Buoyant_Arrival.html) (Shuji)*

## Everyone Has a Price
Make a Negotiation (Precision) Scheme action against a target's Humanity divided by 10, offering a bribe to make them accept it or leave the scene.
*L5R analogue: [Everyone Has a Price](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Everyone_Has_a_Price.html) (Shuji)*

## Field Fortifications
Make a TN 3 Command (Resilience) check as a downtime activity or Support action during mass battle to erect defensive structures that reduce attrition or provide terrain.
*L5R analogue: [Fortress of Necessity](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Fortress_of_Necessity.html) (Shuji)*

## Get Under Their Skin
When making a Social skill check (Power) targeting one or more characters, spend opportunities to give them the Dazed or Enraged condition.
*L5R analogue: [Fanning the Flames](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Fanning_the_Flames.html) (Shuji)*

## Intimidation
Make a TN 4 Command or Performance (Power) Scheme action targeting any number of characters to make them suffer Strife when targeting you with Social skill checks.
*L5R analogue: [All Shall Fear Me](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/All_Shall_Fear_Me.html) (Shuji)*

## Just a Joke
When making a Social skill check using Swiftness, spend opportunities to regain Humanity you lost by saying something crass, rude, or provocative.
*L5R analogue: [All In Jest](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/All_in_Jest.html) (Shuji)*

## Keep Your Cool
Once per scene as a Support action, spend 1 Fortune point to remove Strife equal to your Fame divided by 10 (minimum 1).
*L5R analogue: [Courtier’s Resolve](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Courtier’s_Resolve.html) (Shuji)*

## Lighten the Mood
When making a Social skill check using Swiftness during a game or lighthearted activity, spend opportunities to remove Strife from participants or observers.
*L5R analogue: [Fun and Games](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Fun_and_Games.html) (Shuji)*

## Look Out!
When making a check to resist an effect, spend opportunities to allow one character per opportunity to switch to a posture of their choice.
*L5R analogue: [Look Out!](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Look_Out.html) (Shuji)*

## Manners Maketh the Man
Make a Command or Culture (Resilience) Scheme action against a target's Vigilance to claim protection for an individual, making targeting them cost the attacker both Humanity and Strife.
*L5R analogue: [Civility Foremost](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Civility_Foremost.html) (Shuji)*

## Marching Orders
Make a TN 4 Performance (Swiftness) check during downtime targeting an army or group of characters to allow them to cover normal travel distance regardless of terrain penalties.
*L5R analogue: [Coursing March Chant](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Coursing_March_Chant.html) (Shuji)*

## Mentorship
During downtime or once per session as a Support action, make a TN 5 Command (Resilience) check targeting a character with fewer technique ranks to let them learn a technique you know.
*L5R analogue: [Mentor's Guidance](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Mentor's_Guidance.html) (Shuji)*

## Misdirection
Once per scene, make a Deception (Swiftness) Scheme action against a target's Vigilance to hide your true goal and gain momentum toward a relevant objective during intrigue.
*L5R analogue: [Hidden in Smoke](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Hidden_in_Smoke.html) (Shuji)*

## Negotiation Tactics
When making a Negotiation (Fortune) check to negotiate job payment, spend opportunities to convince the client to pay 10% of the fee up front.
*L5R analogue: [Fluent Bargaining](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Fluent_Bargaining.html) (Shuji)*

## Operational Insight
Once per session, when recalling information, solving a puzzle, or understanding a situation, spend opportunities to gain hints, learn character advantages or techniques, or ask yes-or-no questions.
*L5R analogue: [Lord Togashi’s Insight](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lord_Togashi’s_Insight_(Dragon).html) (Shuji)*

## Perception is Reality
Make a Culture, Command, or Performance (Swiftness) Scheme or Support action against a target's Vigilance to invert one of their known advantages or disadvantages.
*L5R analogue: [Ebb and Flow](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Ebb_and_Flow.html) (Shuji)*

## Prepared for Anything
Make a TN 1 Crafting (Fortune) Support action to recall preparing an item with rarity equal to 1 plus bonus successes, then spend 1 Fortune point to produce it.
*L5R analogue: [Foreseen Need](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Foreseen_Need.html) (Shuji)*

## Provocative Challenge
Once per scene, make a Performance or Tactics (Power) Scheme action against a target's Vigilance to inflict Strife or force them to move toward you or attack your cohort.
*L5R analogue: [Goading Taunt](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Goading_Taunt.html) (Shuji)*

## Read the Room
Make an Insight (Precision) check against a target's Vigilance to learn their ranks in chosen skills and their mental-related advantages or disadvantages.
*L5R analogue: [Assess Strengths](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Assess_Strengths.html) (Shuji)*

## Roast
Once per session, make a TN 3 Performance (Power) Scheme action targeting up to your Power value in characters to inflict Strife and force a Resolve check.
*L5R analogue: [Crackling Laughter](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Crackling_Laughter.html) (Shuji)*

## Show Off
When making an Arts or Performance (Power) check, spend opportunities to increase the next Fame award you receive by 1 per opportunity.
*L5R analogue: [Dazzling Performance](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Dazzling_Performance.html) (Shuji)*

## Slip of the Tongue
Make an Insight (Precision) Scheme action against a target's Focus minus Strife to make them admit something they were concealing.
*L5R analogue: [Cunning Omission](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Cunning_Omission.html) (Shuji)*

## Social Manipulation
Once per scene, make a Culture (Swiftness) or Tactics (Swiftness) Scheme and Support action against the highest Vigilance of two targets to increase one's TN by 2 and decrease the other's by 2.
*L5R analogue: [Flowering Deceptions](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Flowering_Deceptions.html) (Shuji)*

## Strategic Insight
Once per session at scene start or as a Support action, make a TN 2 Science (Fortune) check targeting a character to reduce their Vigilance by 2 and increase their Focus by 2.
*L5R analogue: [Illuminate the Way](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Illuminate_the_Way.html) (Shuji)*

## Tactical Assessment
Make a TN 2 Insight (Resilience) Support action to appraise a character's weaknesses and prevent them from applying one chosen disadvantage until end of scene.
*L5R analogue: [Tactical Assessment](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Tactical_Assessment.html) (Kata)*

## Two Heads Are Better Than One
Make a TN 3 Command (Swiftness) Support action targeting another character to grant mutual assistance on the next checks you both make.
*L5R analogue: [Complementary Experience](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Complementary_Experience.html) (Shuji)*

## Um, Actually
Make a Science (Resilience) Scheme action against a target's Focus minus Strife to silence them or force them to gain Strife.
*L5R analogue: [Cite the Facts](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Cite_the_Facts.html) (Shuji)*

## War Cry
Once per scene as a Support action, make a TN 2 Command (Resilience) check targeting a character within earshot to remove their Strife or Fatigue if it exceeds their thresholds.
*L5R analogue: [Bellow of Resolve](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Bellow_of_Resolve.html) (Shuji)*

# Street

## Alibi
When making Stealth, Subterfuge, or Deception checks (Precision or Swiftness) during downtime surveillance, spend opportunities to create Contrary evidence per opportunity spent.
*L5R analogue: [Artful Alibi](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Artful_Alibi.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Call the Wild
Make a Survival (Power) check against an animal companion's Vigilance to increase their damage and deadliness by 1 plus 1 per two bonus successes, grant them the Enraged condition, and remove their Strife.
*L5R analogue: [Call the Wild](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Call_the_Wild.html) (Shuji)*

## Clean Hands
Spend opportunities on an attack check to reduce the perceived severity of lethal action to observers with lower Vigilance than opportunities spent.
*L5R analogue: [Deceitful Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Deceitful_Strike.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Field Recon
Make a TN 3 Survival (Swiftness) check to learn terrain qualities and fortification locations in an area while remaining concealed from characters with lower Vigilance.
*L5R analogue: [Swift Scouting](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Swift_Scouting.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Friends in Low Places
Once per session, make a TN 2 Subterfuge (Fortune) check to reveal an informant who can provide information on a topic of your choice.
*L5R analogue: [Lord Bayushi’s Whispers](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lord_Bayushi’s_Whispers_(Scorpion).html) (Shuji)*

## Ghost Step
Make a TN 2 Conditioning (Swiftness) check to move one range band plus additional bands per two bonus successes, ignoring terrain movement penalties and crossing vertical surfaces.
*L5R analogue: [Like a Ghost](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Like_a_Ghost.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Improvised Hazard
Make a TN 2 Survival (Swiftness) check to apply one terrain quality (Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring) within 1 range band of a target for one round plus additional rounds per bonus successes.
*L5R analogue: [Chaotic Scattering](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-kata/Chaotic_Scattering.html) (Kata)*

## Phantom Step
Spend opportunities on Initiative or Swiftness Attack checks to cause characters with equal or lower Vigilance to lose track of your position in concealing terrain or crowds.
*L5R analogue: [Skulk](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Skulk.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Poison Blade
Make a TN 3 Blades (Precision) check to apply a poison or drug through a melee attack against a target at range 0, dealing weapon deadliness plus bonus successes as damage.
*L5R analogue: [Deadly Sting](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Deadly_Sting.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Prepared Ambush
Make a TN 4 Conditioning (Resilience) check to conceal yourself and up to your Tactics ranks of allies from detection, lasting until end of your next turn or until they act.
*L5R analogue: [The Patient Viper](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/The_Patient_Viper.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Scrub
Spend opportunities on a Subterfuge check (Precision or Swiftness) to increase the TN to detect or investigate altered incriminating material.

## Silent Assassin
Spend opportunities on any Street technique check to increase the TN of the next check to resist your critical strikes by 1 per opportunity spent.
*L5R analogue: [Stillness of Death](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Stillness_of_Death.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Silent Strike
Make a TN 4 Precision Attack check to deal weapon damage, inflict the Silenced condition, and prevent characters at range 3+ from noticing the strike for one round plus bonus successes.
*L5R analogue: [Silencing Stroke](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Silencing_Stroke.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Vanishing Act
Make a TN 5 Survival (Swiftness) check to conceal yourself and up to your Tactics ranks of allies in Entangling or Obscuring terrain, preventing detection and attacks until end of your next turn or until an Attack action is taken.
*L5R analogue: [Vanish in the Shadows](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Vanish_in_the_Shadows.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## Volatile Dispersal
Expend two substance doses and make a TN 2 Science (Swiftness) check to deal 3 damage to all characters at range 0-2 and force them to resist the substance's effects.
*L5R analogue: [Noxious Cloud](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/Noxious_Cloud.html) (Ninjutsu)*

## What's Yours Is Mine
Make a TN 3 Power Attack and Movement check to deal weapon damage and steal an item from a Dazed target, or move while disarming them.
*L5R analogue: [What's Yours Is Mine](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-ninjutsu/What's_Yours_is_Mine.html) (Ninjutsu)*

# Vehicle

## Rapid Transit
Make a TN 2 Piloting (Fortune) check as a downtime activity to reach a destination in half the normal time, with passengers automatically included.
*L5R analogue: [Lady Shinjo’s Speed](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lady_Shinjo’s_Speed_(Unicorn).html) (Shuji)*
