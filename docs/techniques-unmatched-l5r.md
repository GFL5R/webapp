# Unmatched L5R Techniques

These L5R techniques have no GFL5R analogue. They may represent mechanics
unique to L5R's setting (e.g., shugenja invocations, kiho, courtier shuji)
or simply techniques that don't have a close mechanical equivalent in GFL5R.

---

## Core Techniques Invocations

### [Amaterasu's Gaze](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Amaterasu's_Gaze.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 1 Theology (Fire) check targeting one character or item of silhouette 2 or lower at range 0–3 to summon a ray of sunlight that provides light at range 0–2 of the target's position for one round plus additional rounds equal to bonus successes, with Fire (op)+ extending the light range, Fire (op)+ increasing the target's silhouette limit, Fire (op) (op) providing warmth, and Fire (op) (op) negating Obscuring terrain.

### [Armor of Earth](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Armor_of_Earth.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Earth) check to summon a suit of Cumbersome Wargear that grants physical resistance equal to your Earth Ring plus bonus successes and persists for a number of rounds equal to your Earth Ring, with opportunities to extend the duration to the end of the scene, add the Durable quality, or add the Sacred quality.

### [Armor of Radiance](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Armor_of_Radiance.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Fire) check targeting one set of armor at range 0–1 to augment it with blinding radiance that causes characters at range 0–1 of the wearer to suffer the Burning and Dazed conditions at the start of their turn for one round plus bonus successes, with Fire (op) extending the duration to the end of the scene.

### [Bind the Shadow](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Bind_the_Shadow.html)
As an Attack action, make a TN 3 Theology (Earth) check against one Otherworldly being at range 0–2 to inflict the Immobilized and Silenced conditions on a failed TN 4 Fitness (Air 2, Water 5) resistance check for a duration equal to your Earth Ring, with Earth opportunity spends increasing the resistance TN or extending the binding duration from the end of the scene to one hundred years.

### [Biting Steel](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Biting_Steel.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Fire) check targeting one weapon at range 0–1 to augment it with blazing energy that increases its base damage by your Fire Ring for one round plus additional rounds equal to bonus successes, and spend Fire opportunities to immediately Strike with the readied weapon or increase its deadliness by 1 per opportunity spent.

### [Blessed Wind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Blessed_Wind.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Air) check targeting one position at range 0–3 to summon a swirling wind that grants characters at range 0–2 of that position Obscuring terrain for Attack actions, persisting for one round plus bonus successes or until the end of the scene with an Air opportunity spend.

### [Bond of the Realms](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Bond_of_the_Realms.html)
As an Attack and Scheme action targeting a character from a different realm, make a TN 2 Theology (Earth) check and name their realm of origin; if successful, smite the target to reduce their endurance and composure by 3 (to a minimum of 1) for one round plus bonus successes, optionally applying a condition and extending the duration or increasing damage with Earth opportunities.

### [Breath of the Fire Dragon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Breath_of_the_Fire_Dragon.html)
As an Attack action, make a TN 4 Theology (Fire) check to exhale searing flames that inflict supernatural damage and the Burning condition on a target at range 0–3, forcing nearby characters to make Fitness checks or suffer damage, with Fire opportunity spends allowing additional Dazed conditions, strife, and increased critical strike severity.

### [By the Light of the Lord Moon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/By_the_Light_of_the_Lord_Moon.html)
As a Scheme action, make a TN 2 Theology (Air) check targeting an area at range 0–2 to scry and reveal hidden characters and mundane concealed objects with an illusory outline, spending Air opportunities to reveal one magically concealed object or allow an additional character at range 0–1 to see the revealed items.

### [Bō of Water](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Bō_of_Water.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 1 Theology (Water) check to summon a staff with the Snaring quality that persists until the end of the scene or when it leaves your grip, and spend Water opportunities to transform it into any melee or ranged weapon, perform an immediate Strike action, or adjust its range by one band.

### [Call Upon the Wind](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Call_Upon_the_Wind.html)
As a Movement action, make a TN 4 Theology (Air) check to fly freely, ignore terrain, and carry one additional willing character at range 0–2 per Air opportunity spent.

### [Caress of Earth](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Caress_of_Earth.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 3 Theology (Earth) check targeting one item with the Damaged or Destroyed quality at range 0–1 to mend it and remove that quality, or spend an Earth opportunity to grant one target the Durable quality until the end of the scene.

### [Cloak of Night](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Cloak_of_Night.html)
As a Scheme and Support action, make a Theology (Air) check targeting one object or character at range 0–1 with a TN equal to the target's silhouette to render it invisible to the naked eye for one round plus additional rounds equal to bonus successes, spending Air opportunities to choose one additional target of the same silhouette or smaller per opportunity spent.

### [Courage of Seven Thunders](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Courage_of_Seven_Thunders.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Earth) check targeting up to your Earth Ring characters at range 0–2 to augment each target with vigorous resolve, increasing their composure by your Earth Ring until the end of the scene, and optionally increase their endurance or remove strife by spending Earth opportunities.

### [Dance of Seasons](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Dance_of_Seasons.html)
As a Movement and Support action, make a TN 3 Theology (Water) check targeting a water source at range 1–3 to transform it into Dangerous ice, Obscuring fog, or Entangling mud at range 0–1, spending Water opportunities to add additional target positions or encase characters in ice requiring a TN 3 Fitness resistance to avoid Immobilized and Disoriented conditions.

### [Dominion of Suijin](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Dominion_of_Suijin.html)
As a Movement and Scheme action, make a TN 2 Theology (Water) check targeting a body of water at range 0–1 to scry in its ripples and display a chosen familiar body of water at range 0–6, optionally using clouds or ice as water sources and allowing up to your Water Ring characters to pass through the one-way portal to arrive soaked.

### [Dream Painter](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Dream_Painter.html)
As a Scheme action, make a TN 3 Theology (Air) check against one Unconscious NPC at range 0–3 to scry into their subconscious and discover relevant information, with opportunity spends to afflict them with Exhausted, improve their mood and social TNs, or whisper words into their mind.

### [Earth Becomes Sky](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Earth_Becomes_Sky.html)
As an Attack action, you may make a TN 3 Theology (Earth) check targeting one character at range 1–3; if you succeed, you call forth a boulder to smite the target for supernatural damage equal to your Earth Ring plus bonus successes, requiring a TN 3 Fitness (Air 1, Water 4) resistance check or suffering the Prone condition, with doubled damage if the target is already Prone, and each Otherworldly target within range suffers the Silenced condition until the end of your next turn.

### [Earthquake](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Earthquake.html)
As an Attack action, make a TN 5 Theology (Earth) check targeting each character at range 0–3 to summon an earthquake that forces each target to resist with a TN 5 Fitness check (Air 3, Water 6) at the end of each of your turns or suffer supernatural damage equal to twice your Earth Ring and the Prone condition until the end of the scene, with opportunities to extend the duration, summon a chasm that causes falling, or destroy buildings/fortifications.

### [Emboldened Steed](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Emboldened_Steed.html)
As a Support action targeting any number of mounts at range 0–4, make a TN 3 Theology (Earth) check to augment and mend them, removing Exhausted and strife equal to bonus successes, with Earth opportunities to ignore terrain effects, remove fatigue, and grant automatic success with bonus successes to Movement action checks for riders and mounts until the end of the scene.

### [Embrace of Kenro-Ji-Jin](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Embrace_of_Kenro-Ji-Jin.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Earth) check targeting yourself to gain the ability to stick to and traverse vertical earthen or stone surfaces, persisting for one round plus bonus successes (or one minute narratively), with Earth (op) extending duration to the scene and allowing passage through solid earth plus supernatural resistance to Earth invocations, kihō, and mahō, while Earth (op) (op) extends these benefits to metal weapons and grants physical resistance equal to your Earth Ring.

### [Essence of Jade](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Essence_of_Jade.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 3 Theology (Earth) check to purify an area of the Defiled terrain quality for one round plus bonus rounds, or until the end of the scene if you spend an Earth opportunity, which also grants the Hallowed quality, increases the TN for Tainted beings to resist effects by 2, and causes all damage in the area to have the Sacred quality.

### [Ever-Changing Waves](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Ever-Changing_Waves.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 4 Theology (Water) check to transform your body into a natural animal or supernatural beast of silhouette 2, using that form's physical/social rings and your own mental/spiritual skills until you end the effect or become unconscious, with Water opportunity spends allowing transformation into different sentient species, Otherworldly beings, or adjusting the silhouette by 1 per spend.

### [Extinguish](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Extinguish.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 3 Theology (Fire) check targeting up to three mundane sources of fire at range 0–3 to extinguish them, remove 1 fatigue per source, and end all persistent effects from other Fire invocations and flame-based supernatural effects in the area.

### [False Realm of the Fox Spirits](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/False_Realm_of_the_Fox_Spirits.html)
As a Scheme action, make a TN 5 Theology (Air) check targeting one position at range 0–4 to summon illusory terrain extending up to your Air Ring that fools unaware characters until they resist with a TN 4 Survival check or make physical contact, persisting for rounds equal to your Air Ring plus bonus successes or minutes in narrative/downtime scenes, with Air (op) extending the effect to the end of the scene and Air (op)+ granting the terrain Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring qualities.

### [Fires of Purity](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Fires_of_Purity.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Theology (Fire) check targeting one character at range 0–3 to augment them with sacred fire, causing range bands around them to count as Dangerous terrain for others, while also granting the target fire resistance and Sacred weapon quality, with additional duration or scene-long persistence via bonus successes or opportunity spends.

### [Fluid Shadows](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Fluid_Shadows.html)
As a Movement and Scheme action, make a TN 2 Theology (Water) check targeting a shadowy position at range 1–3 to instantly summon yourself there, spending Water opportunities to increase the technique's maximum range by 1 per opportunity or raise the TN of Attack and Scheme checks targeting you by 2 until the end of your next turn.

### [Fukurokujin’s Wit](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Fukurokujin’s_Wit.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 1 Theology (Fire) check targeting yourself to add a kept (ring) set to an (op) (st) result for one round plus additional rounds equal to bonus successes, with Fire opportunities allowing you to target another character at range 0–2 instead of yourself.

### [Fury of Osano-wo](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Fury_of_Osano-wo.html)

### [Grasp of Earth](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Grasp_of_Earth.html)

### [Grasp of the Air Dragon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Grasp_of_the_Air_Dragon.html)
As an Attack or Support action, make a TN 4 Theology (Air) check to move a target at range 3–5 a number of range bands equal to your Air Ring plus bonus successes (max 6), optionally slamming them to the ground to force a TN 4 Fitness (Earth 5, Fire 2) resistance check against fall damage, with Air opportunity spends increasing the technique's range and the fall's height.

### [Guardian of the Secret Gate](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Guardian_of_the_Secret_Gate.html)

### [Heart of the Lady Sun](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Heart_of_the_Lady_Sun.html)

### [Heart of the Water Dragon](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Heart_of_the_Water_Dragon.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 3 Theology (Water) check targeting one character at range 0–2 to grant them supernatural resistance equal to your Water Ring, remove 1 fatigue at the end of each round, and persist for one round plus additional rounds equal to your bonus successes, with Water opportunities allowing the target to force the technique user to resist the effect of a kihō, mahō, or invocation technique they successfully resist.

### [Inari’s Blessing](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Inari’s_Blessing.html)

### [Jade Strike](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-invocations/Jade_Strike.html)

### [Lord Bayushi’s Whispers](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lord_Bayushi’s_Whispers_(Scorpion).html)

### [Lord Togashi’s Insight](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Lord_Togashi’s_Insight_(Dragon).html)
As an action, make a TN 2 Meditation (Void) check to receive a hint from Togashi regarding a current quandary, and spend Void opportunities to reduce the TN of your first check to overcome that problem by your school rank (to a minimum of 1).

### [Malleable Formation](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Malleable_Formation.html)

### [Moment of Glory](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Moment_of_Glory.html)

### [Offend the Sensibilities](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Offend_the_Sensibilities.html)

### [Osano-wo’s Boast](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Osano-wo’s_Boast_(Mantis).html)

### [Pack Gambit](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Pack_Gambit.html)

### [Pillar of Calm](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Pillar_of_Calm.html)

### [Preserve Strength](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Preserve_Strength.html)
As a Support action or downtime activity, make a TN 3 Medicine (Water) check targeting a friendly army or characters at range 0–3 up to your Command ranks to remove 5 casualties plus 1 per two bonus successes in a mass battle, or reduce the TN of their next Medicine check by 2 plus 1 per two bonus successes in a skirmish, spending Water opportunities to remove Bleeding, Burning, or Dying conditions from a friendly leader in a mass battle or allow targets to move toward such conditions in a skirmish.

### [Prey on the Weak](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Prey_on_the_Weak.html)
As a Scheme action, make a TN 1 Courtesy (Air) check targeting a Compromised character; if successful, the target must either unmask, allowing you to learn one of their disadvantages per Air opportunity spent, or forfeit glory equal to your Air Ring plus bonus successes to leave the scene, allowing you to notice their destination or overhear their retreat per double Air opportunity spent.

### [Rallying Cry](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Rallying_Cry.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 2 Command (Fire) or Performance (Fire) check to energize targets so they add a kept set to an (op) (st) result until the end of your next turn, and spend Fire (op) to inflict 1 strife on each enemy in a skirmish or 1 panic on the enemy army in a mass battle.

### [Regal Bearing](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Regal_Bearing.html)
As a Scheme action, make a Command (Water) or Performance (Water) check with a TN equal to the highest vigilance among your targets to adjust their perceived status by 10 plus or minus 10 per bonus success, persisting until the end of the scene, and spend Water opportunities to learn if a character's actual status is higher or lower than yours.

### [Righteous Example](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Righteous_Example.html)
As an Attack and Support action during a mass battle, spend two Fire opportunities to make a TN 3 Martial Arts (Fire) check targeting an enemy leader's cohort, which on success inflicts attrition and panic on the enemy army and reduces TN for your allies' Attack actions, while on failure reduces TN for enemies targeting your cohort, and if you are also the commander, grants your army 1 momentum point.

### [Roar of Encouragement](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Roar_of_Encouragement.html)
As a Support action, make a TN 3 Command (Fire) check targeting a friendly character to allow them to immediately perform a Strike action during a skirmish or an Assault action during a mass battle with your assistance, spending Fire opportunities to choose additional targets for the same effect.

### [Rouse the Soul](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Rouse_the_Soul.html)
When making a Social (Void) or Theology (Void) check targeting one or more characters, spend Void opportunities to remove one of the following conditions from one target per opportunity spent: Afflicted, Enraged, Exhausted, Intoxicated, or Unconscious.

### [Ruse of the Moon’s Reflection](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Ruse_of_the_Moon's_Reflection.html)
As a Scheme action, make a Command (Air) check with a TN equal to the highest vigilance among your non-hostile targets to exchange the initiative values of those characters during an intrigue, skirmish, or duel, or to reveal that two leaders of the same army have exchanged cohorts and positions during a mass battle.

### [Rustling of Leaves](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Rustling_of_Leaves.html)

### [Sear the Wound](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Sear_the_Wound.html)
When making a Social (Fire) check targeting one or more characters, spend Fire opportunities to choose a known disadvantage of a target per opportunity spent, which applies to all of that target's checks until the end of the scene.

### [Sensational Distraction](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Sensational_Distraction.html)
When making a Social (Fire) check targeting one or more characters, spend Fire opportunities to lower the target's vigilance by 1 per opportunity spent when interacting with others, or raise it by 1 per opportunity spent when interacting with you, with this effect persisting until the end of the scene.

### [Shallow Waters](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Shallow_Waters.html)
When making a Social (Water) check targeting a character, spend Water opportunities to learn of one material item or worldly experience the target desires, or spend two Water opportunities to learn the target's ninjō.

### [Slippery Maneuvers](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Slippery_Maneuvers.html)

### [Spin the Web](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Spin_the_Web_(Kolat).html)

### [Your Enemy’s Arrows](../wiki/L5R Techniques/core-techniques-shuji/Your_Enemy's_Arrows.html)
