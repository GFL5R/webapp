A Boy and His Doll

Character creation in GFL5R builds two characters at once: your human and your T-Doll. These characters form a pair, partners navigating the complexities of 2072's recovering world together. They might be commander and subordinate, handler and agent, friends, or something more complicated. That relationship is yours to define.

The Pair Dynamic

Human

Social capability: connections, influence, understanding of human society, and navigation of the URNC's bureaucratic world.

&

T-Doll

Combat capability: tactical skills, weapon proficiency, and effectiveness in dangerous situations.

These are tendencies, not rules. You can build your pair however you like. What matters is that they complement each other. Where one is weak, the other is strong. Where one hesitates, the other acts. Together, they're greater than the sum of their parts.

Two Perspectives, One Story

Playing a pair lets you engage with the setting from multiple angles:

  • Your human experiences the world as a survivor in an authoritarian system, weighing safety against freedom, navigating corporate and government power.
  • Your T-Doll experiences the world as artificial life claiming personhood, pushing back against being treated as property, finding meaning beyond their programmed purpose.

Both characters have full agency. Both matter equally to the story. Both grow and change through play.
