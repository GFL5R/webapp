Mechanics

GFL5R's core mechanics are derived from Legend of the Five Rings, Fifth Edition (L5R 5E), originally designed by Max Brooke and published by Fantasy Flight Games, later continued by Edge Studio. The roll-and-keep dice engine, the structure of approaches (rings), skills, techniques, advantages, disadvantages, passions (ninjo), anxieties (giri), stances, strife, and the action economy are adapted from L5R 5E with minimal modification.

Subsystems for electronic warfare, vehicles, firearms, collapse radiation, and science are original to GFL5R, designed to extend L5R's framework into a modern military sci-fi context.

The discipline system replaces L5R's school system and draws structural inspiration from the Spheres system by Drop Dead Studios.

Setting

The setting, characters, factions, and lore of GFL5R are drawn from Girls' Frontline and Girls' Frontline 2: Exilium, developed by Sunborn Technology (MICA Team). T-Dolls, the Sangvis Ferri conflict, Collapse radiation, ELID, Griffin & Kryuger, Paradeus, the Neural Cloud architecture, and all named characters referenced in this manual originate from those works.
