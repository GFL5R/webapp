# Wiki Lorebook Index

Reference index for all lorebook files converted from the IOP Wiki. Use this to identify which files to read for specific lore topics, characters, factions, or events.

## Standalone Faction/Organization Files

These are self-contained lorebook entries for major factions and organizations.

### [girard-group.md](girard-group.md)
Powerful industrial conglomerate based in Odesa led by Rurik Girard. Produces Third-Generation Dolls, involved in the Purification Project. Suspected of stealing Persica's Doll technology, linked to the mysterious "Horoscope Program," and issued the bounty contract that delivered Helena to Odesa. Member of the Orlog Conglomerate.

### [griffin-kryuger/](griffin-kryuger/)
The protagonist faction of Girls' Frontline. Private military company founded in 2053 by Kryuger and Griffin Lyons, known for employing Tactical Dolls. Split into multiple files covering history, members, and base life. See subfolder section below.

### [i-o-p.md](i-o-p.md)
IOP (Important Operations Prototype Manufacturing Company), the world's leading Tactical Doll manufacturer directed by Havier Witkin. Traces origins from a Pan-European Alliance military project. Created 16LAB, held near-monopoly after Sangvis Ferri's collapse. Developed the Nidhogg Protocol.

### [kcco.md](kcco.md)
KCCO (Special Operations Forces Command), the Neo-Soviet special forces that became the second antagonist faction under General Carter. Carter allied with Paradeus, engineered the Butterfly Incident of 2061, betrayed G&K during the 2064 Great Retreat, and failed a coup d'etat in Moscow, committing suicide. Includes detailed history of operations and personnel.

### [mangi-security-service.md](mangi-security-service.md)
PMC spun off from Cecht FABN, secretly employing ~2000 mercenaries despite being registered for under 100. Elite unit "Omen" specializes in Paradeus cleanup. Collaborated with Girard Group on Project Coronal Hole. Former G&K Doll Nikketa serves as a mole within them.

### [paradeus.md](paradeus.md)
German terrorist cult and main antagonist faction. Originated from the Evangel Portal doomsday cult, led by the Oberstein family. Created Nyto cyborg soldiers, pursued Relic Technology through experiments on Lunasia. Allied with KCCO's Carter. Largely destroyed in 2064, remnant sects persist in 2074 under "Lady Immore."

### [sangvis-ferri.md](sangvis-ferri.md)
Romanian arms manufacturer turned rogue AI faction. AI mainframe Elisa turned all SF Dolls against humanity in the 2061 Butterfly Incident. Chronicles the faction from founding through dissolution in 2067 when patents entered public domain. Covers the Ringleader hierarchy, M4A1's merger with Elisa, and final fate.

### [union-of-rossartrist-nations-coalition.md](union-of-rossartrist-nations-coalition.md)
The URNC, a global coalition formed in 2064 under Rossartrist ideology that absorbed most nations. Covers the Purification Project, PMC downsizing, the "Ten-Years Honeymoon" technology exchange with Hollow City/Antarctic Union, and the First Antarctic War (2089-2094).

### [varjagers.md](varjagers.md)
Organized bandit faction in Eastern European contamination zones since 2066, using Norse-themed unit names. Became more coordinated in 2074 under Paradeus agent Glasir. Attack Yellow Zone settlements and convoys but avoid contamination sects.

### [gfl2-loading-screens.md](gfl2-loading-screens.md)
Timeline lore snippets from GFL2 loading screens. Key dates: 2030 Beilan Island Incident, 2065 G&K dissolution, 2067 B.R.I.E.F. established, 2067 SF patents public domain, 2071 third-gen Doll technology launched, 2071 Boojums first observed.
