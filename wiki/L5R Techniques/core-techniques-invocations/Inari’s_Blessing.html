Inari’s Blessing

Rank 1

Miya's Gift (Ancestral), Feast of the Water Kami (Elemental), Oasis in the Desert (Foreign)

Water is life, and water kami can provide life-giving gifts of food and drink even from the most desolate environments. After the shugenja performs the invocation, the land nearby flourishes with life, delivering food to the shugenja, and pure water springs from the ground. This food is simple and nourishing, and it can be cooked or even packed away as rations for later if needed.

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a TN 2 Theology (Water) check targeting one position at range 1.

Effects:

If you succeed, you summon a meal at the target position that can feed a number of people up to your Water Ring plus your bonus successes.

New Opportunities

  • Water (op): The meal is particularly fortifying. Any character who partakes of it during downtime removes fatigue equal to your Water Ring.
  • Water (op)+: The meal can feed a number of people up to your Water Ring plus your bonus successes times the (op) spent this way instead.
