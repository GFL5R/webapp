Tempest of Air

Rank 1

Stormy Vial (Alchemy), Howl of Isora (Fortunist), Flap of the Wyvern's Wings (Foreign)

The shugenja beseeches the air spirits to lash out all around them, calling buffeting winds to push foes backward or even hurl them to the ground.

Activation:

As an Attack action, you may make a TN 3 Theology (Air) check targeting each character at range 2–3.

Effects:

If you succeed, blasts of wind smite each target. Each target suffers supernatural damage equal to your Air Ring and must resist with a TN 4 Fitness check (Earth 5, Fire 2) or suffer the Disoriented condition.

New Opportunities

  • Air (op)+: Each target who fails their Fitness check is also pushed 1 range band away from you per (op) spent this way.
