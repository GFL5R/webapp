The World is Not Heavy

Rank 2

Benevolence of the Tidepool (Elemental), Carried by Herd-Back (Foreign), Essence of the Floating World (Alchemy)

When the illusions of the material world are lifted, even temporarily, nothing seems particularly heavy anymore.

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a TN 3 Theology (Water) check targeting yourself or a living creature you are touching with your bare hands.

Effects:

If you succeed, you augment the target's body with the buoyancy of the water kami. The target gains the ability to carry items with a maximum combined silhouette value equal to the target's own silhouette plus 1. Additionally, their armor loses the Cumbersome item quality while they are wearing it. If the target is a steed, the maximum combined silhouette value of riders it can comfortably carry increases by 2—but none of these riders may individually have a silhouette value greater than the steed can normally carry. These effects persist for the duration of the scene.

New Opportunities

  • Water (op)+: Increase the maximum combined silhouette value of items the target can carry by 1 per (op) spent.
  • Water (op) (op): Another item the target has equipped loses the Cumbersome item quality.
  • Water (op) (op): Increase the maximum combined silhouette value of riders the steed can carry by an additional 2.
