Battle in the Mind

Rank 3

As two masters of any martial art square off before a duel, each seeks to predict the other’s angle of attack and strategy, and the ways their predictions might be foiled by the opponent’s.

Activation:

When you make an Initiative check for a duel using your Void Ring, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Void (op): You name two rings, then your opponent must name one of those two rings. When your opponent selects their stance during their first turn of the duel, they cannot select the ring they named as their stance.
