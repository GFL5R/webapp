Bear’s Swipe Style

Rank 1 [Bushi or Monk]

Warriors of many different traditions train to strike for maximum impact, especially when the blow is not meant to disable the foe all on its own, but instead to disorient, distract, or create an opening for a finishing attack. A strike to the temple, knee, or elbow might not end the fight on its own, but if it leaves the opponent reeling and vulnerable, it can still be extremely valuable. Training to make these blows count, then, can be as important to practice as the strike that will end the fight.

Activation:

While you perform an Attack action check, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • Earth (op)(op): Choose one of the following conditions: Bleeding, Dazed, Disoriented, Prone. If this check inflicts that condition on one or more targets, those targets cannot remove that condition until the end of your next turn.
