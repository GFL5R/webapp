Cautious Tread

Rank 1

Horses and camels are excellent mounts, but their long legs make them somewhat more vulnerable to certain kinds of terrain than their human riders. A skillful handler knows how to lead their mount around the most dangerous spots, whether holes dug by rodents in the plains, swampy terrain, or chasms in the desert.

Activation:

When you make a Movement action check while riding a mount, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)(op): Choose one of the following terrain qualities: Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring. You and your mount ignore its effects. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
