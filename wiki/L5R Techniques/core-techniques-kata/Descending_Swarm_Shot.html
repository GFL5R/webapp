Descending Swarm Shot

Rank 3

Just as when one hornet stings its foe, the swarm often follows, so too does the warrior’s first shot lead that of their compatriots. By firing at a key weak point or making allies aware of a target, the leader of a squad can focus their efforts, making the entire unit much more deadly.

Activation:

As an Attack action using a readied bow or sling, you may make a TN 3 Martial Arts [Ranged] (Air) check targeting one character within range of the weapon. Reduce the TN of this check by 1 if your target is of silhouette 3 or larger.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target becomes vulnerable to attacks by your comrades. When a character makes an Attack action check targeting the affected target, if they succeed, they add one bonus success. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)(op): If you succeed, characters attacking the affected target add bonus successes equal to your ranks in Command instead.
  • (op)(op)+: This effect persists for one additional round per (op)(op) spent this way.
