Disappearing World Style

Rank 4

Seeing nothing but their enemy, the bushi surges forward in a flurry of strikes, seeking to land a hit to the eyes, temple, or other vulnerable area rapidly after their initial attack. This stunning blow can leave the enemy staggered, giving the bushi a chance to finish the battle with their next attack.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Melee, Ranged, or Unarmed] (Fire) check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Fire (op)+ : One target of your action must resist with a TN 3 Fitness check (Air 4, Water 1) or suffer the Dazed condition and fatigue equal to their shortfall. Increase the TN of the check to resist by 1 per (op) spent this way.
