Distracting Flurry Style

Rank 2

The land itself can be a weapon to a warrior who chooses to see the opportunities it presents. Snapping brush, loose soil kicked up by a strike, or even sand hurled in desperation can catch a foe off guard, creating a brief moment of vulnerability.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check using Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed], if you are in Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring terrain, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op): If you fail, your target suffers the Disoriented condition.
