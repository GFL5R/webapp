Fierce Badger Style

Rank 2

The Fierce Badger Style relies on controlling an enemy's position on the sumai mat and battlefield alike. It lets the combatant hold their opponent in check until they collapse, or, if desired, hurl them outside the designated area—or off a nearby cliff.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Unarmed] or Fitness check, you may spend (op) in the following ways:

  • (op)+: If the target is Immobilized, they receive 1 strife per spent this way.
  • (op)+: If the target's silhouette and vigilance both are lower than or equal to (op) spent this way, the target is thrown 2 range bands in a direction of your choice and suffers 3 fatigue.
