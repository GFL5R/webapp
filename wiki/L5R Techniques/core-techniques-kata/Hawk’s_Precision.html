Hawk’s Precision

Rank 1

While most bushi are expected to be proficient in the use of the bow, some are terrifyingly accurate, capable of hitting targets well beyond the expected engagement range.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Ranged] check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • (op)+ : Treat the range of the weapon as 1 higher per (op) spent this way, to a maximum of range 6.
