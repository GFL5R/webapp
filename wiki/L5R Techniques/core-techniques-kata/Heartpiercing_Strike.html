Heartpiercing Strike

Rank 3

Battle is the art of killing, and woe betide any warrior who forgets it. With a roar, the bushi launches forward, stabbing out with their weapon to fatally impale their enemy. Even if the strike does not slay the foe, it is likely to weaken them significantly.

Activation:

As an Attack and Movement action using a readied weapon, you may make a TN 4 Martial Arts (Fire) check using the appropriate skill for that weapon, targeting one character within the weapon\u2019s range.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target suffers a critical strike with severity equal to your weapon\u2019s deadliness plus your bonus successes. The TN of the Fitness check to resist this critical strike is 3 (Air 4, Water 1) instead of the normal TN. If you fail, you suffer the Disoriented condition.

New Opportunities:

  • Fire (op): Before resolving the effect, move up to 1 range band toward your target.
