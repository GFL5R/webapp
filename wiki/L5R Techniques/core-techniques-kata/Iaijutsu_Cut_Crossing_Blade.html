Iaijutsu Cut: Crossing Blade

Rank 2

The bushi twists their scabbard outward as they draw, cutting horizontally to extend their reach without being cut themselves.

Activation:

As an Attack action using one sheathed Razor-Edged weapon, you may make a TN 3 Martial Arts [Melee] check targeting one character at range 0–1.

Effects:

You draw and ready the sheathed weapon in a one-handed grip.

If you succeed, you deal physical damage to the target equal to the weapon’s deadliness plus your bonus successes.

New Opportunities:

  • (op) : Ready one other sheathed Razor-Edged weapon.
