Iron Shell Style

Rank 3

To defend against a single, predictable blow, a warrior can dig in, rooting themselves and dispersing the force as it comes. Such a complete defense is exhausting to maintain, but it can keep a fighter on their feet when otherwise they would surely have fallen.

Activation:

When you perform a Guard action, if you have not moved any range bands this turn, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Earth or Void (op)(op)+ : Choose one character you can perceive per (op)(op) spent this way. The next time you defend against physical damage from that character, your fatigue cannot be increased to a value greater than your endurance. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
