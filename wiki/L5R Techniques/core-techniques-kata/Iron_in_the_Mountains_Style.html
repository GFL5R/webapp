Iron in the Mountains Style

Rank 4

The bushi strikes with crushing force, breaking their enemy’s footing and knocking them to the ground with an overwhelming blow or sweeping their stance out from underneath them with a well-placed kick.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Melee, Ranged, or Unarmed] (Earth) check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

New Opportunities:

  • Earth (op)+ : One target of your action must resist with a TN 3 Fitness check (Air 1, Water 4) or suffer the Prone condition and fatigue equal to their shortfall. Increase the TN of the check to resist by 1 per (op) spent this way.
