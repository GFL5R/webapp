Lord Shiba’s Valor (Phoenix)

Rank 2

Once, in the Shadowlands, the Kami Shiba stood alone between a vast horde of its foul denizens and the last of the Seven Thunders after their victory against Fu Leng. Drawing the legendary sword Ofushikai, Shiba cut his way through the host to the First Oni. Legends say that their blades flashed for a hundred days, shattering mountains around them and cutting great rifts across the ground. In the end, each slew the other, but Shiba fell with his oath to protect unbroken.

Activation:

Once per game session, as a Support action using one readied weapon, you may make a TN 2 Tactics (Void)

Effects:

If you succeed, other characters cannot select your targets as the targets of their Attack or Scheme actions if they could select you instead. If an action has multiple targets, you must be the first target chosen. This effect persists for a number of rounds equal to your school rank.

New Opportunities:

Void (op): If you succeed, increase the TN of Attack and Scheme checks targeting you by 1. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
