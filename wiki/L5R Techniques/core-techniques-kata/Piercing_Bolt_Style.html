Piercing Bolt Style

Rank 3

Long-hafted weapons have the advantage of reach, but this does not mean that mobility cannot play a key role in fighting styles built around a spear or ax. A slash or jab that might only cause a light wound from a standing position becomes far deadlier with the weight of a charge or throw behind it.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check using an ax or polearm, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • (op): Treat the deadliness of your weapon as increased by the number of range bands you have traveled this round, and if you threw it, add the number of range bands it has traveled this round.
