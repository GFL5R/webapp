Scouring Wind Style

Rank 3

As the wind in the desert carves the rocks over generations, so the warrior’s weapon carves through their opponent’s defenses. By hacking smoothly and repeatedly, the warrior’s sustained onslaught eventually creates a gap through which they can deliver a fatal blow.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check using Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed] while wielding a non-Razor-Edged weapon in two hands, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)(op): If you succeed, instead of dealing damage, choose one defensive item your target is using (such as armor they are wearing or a readied shield they are wielding). The item receives the Damaged item quality; if it is already Damaged, it becomes Destroyed instead.
  • (op)(op): If you succeed and you are wielding an ax, in addition to dealing damage, choose one defensive item your target is using (such as armor they are wearing or a readied shield they are wielding). It receives the Damaged item quality; if it is already Damaged, it becomes Destroyed instead.
