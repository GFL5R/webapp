Shattering Tide Style

Rank 3

It is rare that a samurai can pick the site of a battle, and battles can occur in locations where no actual fighting is needed for harm to come to all parties. Where the environment is as hazardous as sharp steel or heavy fists, learning ways to incorporate such terrain into attacks makes each strike all the more effective.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Unarmed] or Fitness check, you may spend (op) in the following ways:

  • (op)(op)+: If you succeed, and both you and your target are in Confining or Dangerous terrain, treat the damage and deadliness of the weapon you used as 1 higher for each kept result on your check.
  • (op)(op)+: If both you and your target are in Confining or Dangerous terrain, your target suffers the Dazed condition.
