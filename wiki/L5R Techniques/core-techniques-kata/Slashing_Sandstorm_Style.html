Slashing Sandstorm Style

Rank 2

Thanks to the weight of its head, the length of its shaft, and its cutting edge, an ax generates incredible leverage in battle, potentially splitting skulls or severing limbs. In a whirl of blows, the warrior drives their foes before them, cutting down the weak and testing the resolve of the strong.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check using Martial Arts [Melee] while wielding an ax, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op): If you succeed and your target is a minion, your target suffers additional damage equal to the deadliness of your weapon.
  • (op)+: If you fail and your target’s ranks in Meditation are less than or equal to (op) spent this way, your target receives strife equal to the deadliness of your weapon.
