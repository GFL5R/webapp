Staggering Shot

Rank 2

Putting an arrow at a foe’s feet can cause them to stumble or trip, and an experienced archer knows how to take full advantage of this, planting bolts to break an enemy’s stride or pinning their clothes to slow them down, making any approach extremely treacherous.

Activation:

When you perform a Martial Arts [Ranged] check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Earth or Fire (op)(op): Your target treats all terrain as having the Dangerous quality. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
  • Air or Water (op)(op): Your target treats all terrain as having the Entangling quality. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
