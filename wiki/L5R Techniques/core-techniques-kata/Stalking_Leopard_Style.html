Stalking Leopard Style

Rank 2

The Ujik hunt and herd as their way of life, and one of the deadliest animals in their domain with which they must contend is the leopard. Silent and precise in its strikes, this creature is emulated by some of the most skillful Ujik hunters.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)(op): If you succeed and your target is Disoriented or Prone, treat the damage and deadliness of your weapon as increased by your ranks in Survival.
