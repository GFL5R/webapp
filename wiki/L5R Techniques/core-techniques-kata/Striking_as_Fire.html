Striking as Fire

Rank 1

Victory cannot be achieved through defense alone, and it is a poor student of the sword who forgets that their most important goal is simple: cut down their enemy. All else fades to the background as the bushi fiercely presses the attack, trying to force an error or open an opportunity to end their foe.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Melee, Ranged, or Unarmed] (Fire) Attack action check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Fire (op)+ : The next time your target suffers a critical strike, increase its severity by 1 per (op) spent this way. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
