Sudden Downpour Style

Rank 3

Like the rains sweeping across the steppe, the warrior rides down their foe with the full power of their mount behind their strikes.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check using a bow or polearm, if you are mounted, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)(op): If your target makes a check to resist an effect of this Attack action (including a check to resist a critical strike you inflict as part of the attack), increase the TN of that check by your mount’s Water ring.
