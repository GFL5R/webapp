Swallow’s Dart Style

Rank 2 [Bushi or Monk]

A truly great sword technique might be described as being able to catch a swallow in flight. A warrior who sidesteps as unexpectedly as a flitting bird is an extremely difficult target, as well. However, against multiple blows, this sort of focused evasion can be overcome, and the more weight a fighter is carrying in their armor, the more difficult it is to move this nimbly.

Activation:

While you perform a Movement action check, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • Air (op)(op): The next time you defend against damage, reduce the damage you suffer by an amount equal to your focus minus the physical resistance of your armor instead of by your physical or supernatural resistance. This effect persists until the start of your next turn.
