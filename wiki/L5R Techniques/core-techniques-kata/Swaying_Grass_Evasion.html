Swaying Grass Evasion

Rank 3

Whether traveling the Plains of Wind and Stone or riding the Sand Road, there is no more dangerous place to be caught than out in the open without a mount. Warriors experienced in fighting in these regions know to watch for key pieces of terrain, as even a small amount of cover can mean the difference between life and being shot full of arrows.

Activation:

As a Movement and Support action, if you are in Dangerous, Entangling, or Obscuring terrain, you may receive a number of fatigue up to your ranks in Survival to duck into cover to avoid oncoming threats.

Effects:

Increase the TN of Martial Arts [Ranged] checks targeting you by half the amount of fatigue you received this way (rounded up). This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
