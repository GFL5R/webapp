Swirling Tempest Style

Rank 5

A spear or club’s reach not only allows it to threaten foes at a distance, but also gives its wielder the ability to engage more foes, threatening numerous enemies simultaneously with a furious crash of blows.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Melee] check with a blunt weapon or polearm, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • (op)+: Choose one character in range of your weapon other than your target with vigilance lower than or equal to (op) spent this way. That character suffers physical damage equal to your weapon’s base damage. You may activate this (op) multiple times, choosing a different character each time.
