Thicket's Embrace Style

Rank 3

A warrior must always remember that there are two opponents they must face in combat: their foe, and their environment. It might be easy to chop away thick vegetation covering the terrain, but a true master uses the territory to their advantage. By becoming one with the environment, the warrior can concentrate more on their foe and thus end a fight much faster.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Unarmed] or Fit:ness check, you may spend (op) in the following ways:

  • (op)+: If you are in Entangling or Obscuring terrain, reduce the TN of the next attack check you make by 1 (to a minimum of 1). This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
  • (op)+: If you are in Entangling or Obscuring terrain, you may ignore its negative effects. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
