Thunderbolt Style

Rank 4

Hitting a target with a thrown weapon is difficult; hitting more than one target with the same throw is even harder. However, a warrior of sufficient skill can potentially harm multiple targets with a properly placed spear or ax throw, the projectile striking one foe and caroming off at an unexpected angle or causing the primary target to collapse into another. Such an unexpected tactic can catch an entire unit off guard, forcing them to spread out when staying close together would be advantageous.

Activation:

When you make an Attack action check in which you throw your weapon, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)+: Choose another character at range 0–1 of your target with vigilance lower than your ranks in Fitness plus (op) spent this way. That character suffers damage equal to your weapon’s base damage. You can activate this opportunity multiple times, choosing different characters to affect each time.
