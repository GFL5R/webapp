Trip the Leg

Rank 1

The bushi’s spear flashes out, aiming low to trip up their opponent and send the unlucky foe crashing to the ground.

Activation:

As an Attack action using one readied polearm, you may make a TN 2 Martial Arts [Melee] check targeting one character at range 1–2.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target suffers the Prone condition.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)+ : If your target’s vigilance is lower than or equal to the amount of (op) spent this way, they suffer the Disoriented condition.
  • (op) : If you succeed, the target receives 2 fatigue and 2 strife.
