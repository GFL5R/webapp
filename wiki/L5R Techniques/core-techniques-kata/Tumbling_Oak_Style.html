Tumbling Oak Style

Rank 4

The warrior strikes with decisiveness, leveraging down their weapon in a mighty arc to generate the force needed to end the fight. While such blows can be easy to avoid, they are much easier to land on a ground target and are very difficult to resist with toughness alone.

Activation:

While you make an Attack action check using a staff (such as a bō) or a blunt weapon, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)+: If this check causes one or more targets to make a check to resist a condition, critical strike, or other effect, increase the TN of that check by 2 per (op) spent this way.
