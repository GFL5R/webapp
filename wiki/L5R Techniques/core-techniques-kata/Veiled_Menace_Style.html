Veiled Menace Style

Rank 2

Assassination and other clandestine tactics are frowned upon by many samurai. None can deny the effectiveness of such techniques, however. By drawing their enemy’s gaze away from the true threat, a bushi can often tip the scales against a strong foe.

Activation:

When you make a Martial Arts [Melee] or Martial Arts [Unarmed] Attack action check using a weapon in a one-handed grip, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • (op)+ : If you succeed, choose one unaware or Disoriented target of your action. That target suffers a critical strike with severity equal to your weapon’s deadliness plus 1 per (op) spent this way beyond the first.
