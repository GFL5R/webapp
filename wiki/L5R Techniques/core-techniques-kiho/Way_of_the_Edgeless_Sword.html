Way of the Edgeless Sword

Rank 5

The wise know that it is not the blade that cuts, but the swordmaster. After all, the Tao of Shinsei teaches that weapons are simply another tool, no more sacred than a harvesting thresher or plow. The monk runs their hand along an object, channeling ki into it and transforming it into a killing edge, even if it appears to be nothing more than a common stick, a wooden sword, or their wizened hand.

Activation:

As an Attack and Support action, you may make a TN 3 Meditation check to empower your blows with unarmed and improvised weapons. When you perform this action, you may choose any number of characters at range 0–1 as targets.

Enhancement Effect:

If you succeed, this kihō activates. While this kihō is active, treat the base damage and deadliness of your unarmed profile and improvised weapons you wield as being equal to your ranks in Martial Arts [Unarmed] plus your Void Ring. Additionally, they count as possessing the Durable and Razor-Edged qualities.

Burst Effect:

If you have two or more bonus successes, each target suffers a critical strike with deadliness equal to your Void Ring plus your ranks in Martial Arts [Unarmed].
