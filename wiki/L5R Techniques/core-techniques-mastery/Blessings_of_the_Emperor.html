Blessings of the Emperor

Mastery Ability

Once per scene as a Scheme action, you may make a TN 4 Command (Air) check targeting a number of characters in the scene up to your glory rank. If you succeed, until the end of the scene, the targets must forfeit honor equal to twice their honor rank and glory equal to twice their glory rank before choosing you as the target of an Attack action, and increase the TN of their Attack actions targeting you by 2. This effect persists until the end of the scene, or until you attack an affected character or their allies.

(op)+ : Choose one ally per (op) spent this way. The effects of this ability also apply to targets’ attacks against the chosen allies.
