Little Truths

Mastery Ability

When you perform a check targeting another character to which one of your advantages or disadvantages applies, you may choose another advantage or disadvantage that shares a type with it (see Advantages and Disadvantages, on page 99). Unless they have clear evidence to the contrary, the target comes to believe that you have the advantage or disadvantage you chose instead of the one you actually have.

If the target attempts to seize or exploit this false advantage or disadvantage (see page 101), their check automatically counts as failing with a shortfall of 3. They suffer 3 strife and the Disoriented condition.
