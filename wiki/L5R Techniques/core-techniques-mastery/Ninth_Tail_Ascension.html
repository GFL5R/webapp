Ninth Tail Ascension

Mastery Ability

Your true form now has nine tails. While you are in your true form, your supernatural resistance and physical resistance each increase by 2, and when you make a skill check, you may alter the result of one die containing (su) to a result containing (ex). While you are in your true form, your Attack action checks gain the following new opportunity:

(op) (op): After resolving this action, if your target is Incapacitated or Unconscious, you devour or banish their body or soul, instantly killing them.
