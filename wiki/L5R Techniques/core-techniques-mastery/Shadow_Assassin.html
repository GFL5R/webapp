Shadow Assassin

Mastery Ability

Once per scene as an Attack and Movement action, you may summon a shadow assassin. The shadow has no corporeal form and cannot be harmed. At the end of each of your turns, you may perform a Strike action using one of your readied weapons targeting any character in the scene who is in Obscuring terrain (or other darkness, at the GM's discretion), regardless of range. Damage dealt this way is supernatural. This effect persists until the end of the scene.
