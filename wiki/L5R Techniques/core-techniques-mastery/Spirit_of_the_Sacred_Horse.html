Spirit of the Sacred Horse

Mastery Ability

Any steed you ride gains +4 endurance, +2 composure, +1 vigilance, and the Resplendent item quality. When you spend 1 or more Void points during a Martial Arts [Ranged] or Performance check, an item you have equipped gains the Sacred and Hewing (page 116) item qualities, and you add a number of kept set to (op) results equal to your Void ring.
