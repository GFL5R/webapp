Stir the Slumbering Spirit

Mastery Ability

Once per game session as a downtime activity, you may make a TN 4 Artisan (Void) check to awaken an item’s spirit. If you succeed, until the end of the game session, it becomes a nemuranai (see page 307) with a sealed invocation of your choice based on the item’s history. The GM is the final arbiter of the appropriateness of any invocation selected this way.
