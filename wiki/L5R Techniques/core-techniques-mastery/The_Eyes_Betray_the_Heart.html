The Eyes Betray the Heart

Mastery Ability

Once per scene when performing a skill check to investigate, you may spend (op) as follows:

(op)+ : You may reserve up to one dropped die per (op) spent this way. The next time you perform a check to interact with the person or thing you were investigating (such as a check targeting that person or a check to use that object), you may add any number of these reserved dice as kept dice. Then, remove any unused reserved dice.
