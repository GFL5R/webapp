The Herd Flourishes Together

Mastery Ability

When you make a Social skill check, you may spend ⟗⟘ as follows:

  • Transfer Momentum: Choose another character's social objective that you know. You may transfer any number of momentum points you have accrued to the completion of their objective.
  • Gain Support: After that character completes their objective, you gain the Support of [That Character's Group] advantage (see page 110 of the core rulebook) if you do not have it already.
