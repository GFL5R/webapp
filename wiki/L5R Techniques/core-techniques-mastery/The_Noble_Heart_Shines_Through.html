The Noble Heart Shines Through

Mastery Ability

Once per scene, you may spend 1 Void point to target any non-Shadowlands creature of silhouette 3 or greater, giving the target the Loyal Steed ability—it can be ridden by a human-sized (silhouette 2 or lower) rider—until the end of the scene. When you do, you may make a Courtesy (Water) or Theology (Void) check with a TN equal to the target’s vigilance. If you succeed on the check, the target is favorable toward you and willing to be ridden. Horses of the Blessed Herd—the Utaku steeds—are immune to this mastery ability.
