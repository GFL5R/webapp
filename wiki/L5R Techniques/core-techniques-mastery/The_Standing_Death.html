The Standing Death

Mastery Ability

Once per game session, when you perform the Challenge action (see page 263 of the core rulebook), you may choose up to 6 additional characters who can hear you as targets. These targets must have status lower than or equal to your original target's. If you succeed, each target must fight you in a clash one at a time in an order of their choosing (or flee the battle). Each clash lasts one round. After each clash, you regain fatigue equal to your Earth Ring. Until all of these clashes have concluded (or you have been killed), none of your targets can perform Attack or Scheme actions outside of their individual clashes.
