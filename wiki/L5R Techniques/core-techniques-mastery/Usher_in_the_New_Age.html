Usher In the New Age

Mastery Ability

As a downtime activity, you may overcome or bypass an NPC's guards or other security measures in order to gain access to them. Make a Skulduggery (Air) check targeting the NPC, with a TN equal to their highest conflict rank. If you succeed and the chosen NPC is a minion or an adversary the GM deems non-essential to the campaign plot, you accomplish your goal, such as assassinating the NPC or delivering an effective threat, in a method of your choosing (subject to the GM’s approval). If the NPC is an important adversary, you confront the NPC alone, likely beginning a conflict scene.

(op) (op): Your efforts go completely unnoticed by anyone except possibly the target.

(op) (op): If you kill the target, their death appears to be the result of an accident or natural causes (although a skilled investigator may still uncover the truth).
