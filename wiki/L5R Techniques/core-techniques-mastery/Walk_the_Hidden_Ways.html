Walk the Hidden Ways

Mastery Ability

When you use your school ability in a place already linked to a particular realm, such as a place of power, you may move yourself and a number of characters up to your ranks in Meditation into that realm fully, leaving the Mortal Realm entirely.

As a Support action, you may make a TN 2 Meditation (Void) check to return yourself and a number of characters up to your ranks in Meditation to the Mortal Realm.
