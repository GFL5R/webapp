Rank 1

Shinobi are known for walking silently from shadow to shadow, moving like phantoms. Some of this relies upon agility and speed, of course, but veterans in the art of stealth know that using a crowd or another visual distraction to walk unnoticed can be as effective as the most complex acrobatics.

Activation:

When you perform an Initiative or Attack check using Air, you may spend (op) as follows:

New Opportunities:

  • Air (op): If you are in Obscuring terrain, a crowd, or otherwise concealed from sight, one character with vigilance lower than or equal to your ranks in Skulduggery loses sight of you.
