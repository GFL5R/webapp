Rank 2

Shinobi are never knocked down for long. A trained assassin can bounce back from being prone in a moment, or with a quick roll and acrobatics, always manage to land on their feet.

Activation:

When you make a Martial skill (Water or Earth) check, you may spend (op) in the following way:

New Opportunities:

  • Water (op)+ or Earth (op)+: Remove one existing Disoriented or Prone condition from yourself.
  • Water (op) or Earth (op): Do not suffer the Disoriented and Prone conditions if the check would cause you to suffer them.
