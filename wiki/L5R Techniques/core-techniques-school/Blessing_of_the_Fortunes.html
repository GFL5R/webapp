Blessing of the Fortunes

School Ability

You have gained the goodwill of the Fortune you serve, who grants you certain invocations. When you make a check to use an invocation you learned this way, add additional bonus successes equal to your school rank.

Select a Fortune to be the primary object of your veneration and choose one appropriate invocation (see the sidebar The Fortunes and their Blessings) for which you meet the rank prerequisite. You learn that invocation (without paying its XP cost).

Each time your school rank increases, you gain one additional invocation this way.
