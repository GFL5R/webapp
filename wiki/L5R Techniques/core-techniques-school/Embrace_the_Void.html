Embrace the Void

School Ability

When you spend a Void point to Seize the Moment (see page 36 of the core rulebook), you may also treat your ranks in the skill you are using as being equal to your school rank. If your ranks in the skill are equal to or higher than your school rank, or if you have 5 ranks in the skill, you may add one kept (ring) set to an (op) result instead.
