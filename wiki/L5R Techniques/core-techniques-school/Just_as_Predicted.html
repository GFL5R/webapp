Just as Predicted

School Ability

At the beginning of a game session or as a downtime activity, you may make a TN 1 Theology check using any ring. If you succeed, choose a number of kept dice up to your school rank to reserve. Until the end of the game session or the next downtime scene, when any character makes a skill check using the chosen ring, you may replace one or more kept dice with the same number of reserved dice of the same type.
