Soul of Ceremony

School Ability

When you make a check to perform a ritual, you may add ~> results equal to your school rank. You may spend ~> as if the ritual were also a Social skill check of your choice (such as to activate a shuji, etc.).

Favored Counselor (Mastery Ability)

When making a check to perform a ritual, you may spend ~> as follows:

  • ~>+ : One participant in the ritual with vigilance lower than or equal to ~> spent this way feels especially indebted to you. You gain the Ally [that character] advantage (see page 101 of the core rulebook).
