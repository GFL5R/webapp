The Horse Lord's Favor

School Ability

When you make a check to craft, improve, or maintain an item, you may imprint it with the Horse Lord’s Favor, giving it one of your advantages or disadvantages. If the item is a meishōdō talisman, you may instead give the item an advantage or disadvantage from its sealed spirit. While an item with your Horse Lord’s Favor adorns a horse or a creature with the Loyal Steed ability, the horse or creature is Otherworldly and possesses the chosen advantage or disadvantage. If the item is a meishōdō talisman, the horse or creature may perform any techniques it contains. You can sustain a number of items with your Horse Lord’s Favor up to your school rank.

Additionally, when you perform a Medicine check to remove fatigue or a condition from a horse or other equine creature, add bonus successes equal to your school rank.
