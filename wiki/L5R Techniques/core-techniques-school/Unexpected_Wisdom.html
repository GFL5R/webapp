Unexpected Wisdom

School Ability

You are a wealth of wisdom, which you can dispense a number of times per game session equal to your school rank. On your turn or at any appropriate time during a narrative scene, you may dispense a small bit of wisdom to a character who can hear you and has not benefitted from this ability this scene.
