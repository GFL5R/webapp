Warden of the Five Winds

School Ability

When you make a check to perform the Guard action, you gain a bonus success if you or your target is mounted on a steed. Additionally, when you succeed on a check to perform the Guard action, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)+ : Give a target at range 0–3 the Emboldened condition (see page 133). You can spend a maximum number of (op) this way up to your school rank.
