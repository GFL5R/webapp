A Samurai’s Fate

Rank 4

A leader asks their warriors for the final service all samurai must someday render to their lord: to lay down their lives that others might live. Even as they fall in battle, their shed blood waters the fields that will grow a thousand victories.

Activation:

As an action, you make a TN 5 Command (Void) check targeting any number of characters (or your cohort, during a mass battle).

Effects:

  • If you succeed during a skirmish, each target ignores any critical strikes with severity lower than your Void Ring plus your bonus successes. This effect persists until the end of the scene (at which point the injuries take effect).
  • If you succeed during a mass battle, your army counts as having the Fearless Army ability, below:
  • Fearless Army: At the end of each round, your army removes panic equal to your glory rank. This effect persists until the end of the scene.
