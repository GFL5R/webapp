All Shall Fear Me

Rank 3

If a courtier can pull it off, dominating a gathering through the sheer force of their personality is a good way to ensure everyone will treat them with trepidation and respect. Of course, just being noticed is only half the battle; the courtier must also appear fearsome and even a bit dangerous, but not overdo their act. If they come off as a Kabuki villain, all they’ll get from their audience is a few muffled giggles.

Activation:

As a Scheme action, you may make a TN 4 Command or Performance (Fire) check targeting any number of characters in the scene.

Effects:

If you succeed, each target receives 2 strife when they choose you as a target for a Social skill check. This effect persists until the end of the scene.

If you fail, you receive 4 strife.

New Opportunities:

  • Fire (op) (op)+: If you succeed, all characters targeted by you suffer 1 additional strife per (op) (op) spent this way whenever they target you with a Social skill check.
