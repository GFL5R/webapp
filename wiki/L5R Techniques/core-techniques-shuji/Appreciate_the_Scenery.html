Appreciate the Scenery

Rank 1

In many lands, including Rokugan, the beauty of the natural world inspires art, poetry, and other works of note, even (or perhaps especially) when it is stark and dangerous. When on a long and perilous journey into foreign lands where deadly intrigues surely await, finding beauty in the rougher parts of the world can be a much-needed source of solace.

Activation:

When you make a Support action check targeting another character, if you and the character you are targeting are in Dangerous, Entangling, Halloed, Imbalanced, or Obscuring terrain, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • Earth or Water (op): You and your target each remove strife equal to your ranks in Composition .
  • Air or Fire (op): You and your target gain the Emboldened condition (see page 133).
  • Void (op): You and your target gain the Centered condition (see page 133).
