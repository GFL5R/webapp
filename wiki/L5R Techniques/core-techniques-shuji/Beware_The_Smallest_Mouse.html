Beware The Smallest Mouse

Rank 1 (Water)

Often, minor functionaries at court find themselves able to successfully complete their tasks because they are so unimportant that nobody bothers to impede them (or even notices their existence). Some courtiers quickly learn to play up this appearance when need be.

Activation:

When you make a Social skill (Water) check targeting one or more characters, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Water (op) (op)+: While interacting with you, the target treats their vigilance as 1 lower per (op) (op) spent this way, and decreases the TN of their social checks targeting you by 1. This effect persists until the end of the scene.
