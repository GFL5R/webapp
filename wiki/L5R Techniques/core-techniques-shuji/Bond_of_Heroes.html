Bond of Heroes

Rank 5

Whenever heroes arise—whether they are the Seven Samurai defending a backwater village from bandits—it’s not just their force of arms that makes them legends. Their bonds, their feelings for one another: these are what drive them to great deeds, and why people tell their tales time and again across the ages.

Activation:

Once per game session as a Support action, you may make a TN 5 Performance (Void) check targeting each character who shares a bond with you in the scene.

Effects:

If you succeed, each target removes 5 strife and 5 fatigue, plus 1 for every two bonus successes (see Bonds, on page 187).
