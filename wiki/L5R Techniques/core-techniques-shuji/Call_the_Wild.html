Call the Wild

Rank 3

While an animal or spiritual companion can be a source of comfort and camaraderie for Rokugani, there are times when the untamed beast is needed. Event the bravest of bushi might falter when a great cat bares its claws and makes ready to leap, or a growling wolf pads out of the darkness with blood on its fangs,

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a Survival (Fire) check targeting one friendly animal or manifest kami at range 0-2. The TN of the check is equal to the animal or kami's martial conflict rank.

Effects:

If you succeed, increase the damage and deadliness of the animal or kami's weapon profiles by 1, plus 1 for every two bonus successes beyond the first. This effect persists until the end of the scene.

The animal gains the Enraged condition, then removes all strife.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)+: You may target one additional friendly animal or manifest kami per (op) spent this way.
  • (op)+: The target animal or kami removes 1 fatigue per (op) spent this way.
  • (op): The animal gains the Enraged condition, then removes all strife.
