Call to Ride

Rank 1

A skilled rider can train their loyal steed to come to them on command. When the rider takes a long jour- ney through perilous lands, this technique can be especially vital for escaping the chaos of battle or pursuing marauders who have struck at their caravan.

Activation:

As a Support action, you can make a TN 1 Survival (Water) check targeting your mount or animal companion, provided it is within range 5 of you.

Effects:

If you succeed, your mount or animal companion immediately moves up to 2 range bands toward you, plus 1 additional range band for every two bonus successes. If it is a mount and ends at range 0 of you, you can immediately mount it.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)(op): Increase the TN of Attack action checks targeting you and your mount or animal companion by 1. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.
  • (op)(op): If you perform a Maneuver action after mounting, you may move up to 2 range bands instead of 1.
