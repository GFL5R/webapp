Complementary Experience

Rank 3

Two heads are better than one, or so the saying goes, in part because those two heads do not have identical experiences. By working with someone with a markedly different skill set, a courtier can expand their tactics significantly and find solutions they might never have considered seeking.

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a TN 3 Command (Water) check targeting one other character in the scene.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target has your assistance on the next check they make. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

If you succeed, your target may grant you assistance on the next check you make. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)(op): Choose one skill in which you have 0 ranks and your target has 1 or more ranks. When you grant assistance to another character, you count as being trained in that skill. This effect persists until the end of the scene.
