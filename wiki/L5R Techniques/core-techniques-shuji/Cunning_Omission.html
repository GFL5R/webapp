Cunning Omission

Rank 3

Activation:

As a Scheme action, you may make a Sentiment (Air) check targeting another character. The TN of this check is equal to your target’s focus minus their current strife, to a minimum of 1.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target slips up and admits something they were concealing from you. If your target was not concealing anything from you, you now know this fact with certainty.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)(op)+: If you succeed, your target receives 2 strife per (op) spent this way.
