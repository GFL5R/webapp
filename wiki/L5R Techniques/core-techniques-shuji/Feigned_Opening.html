Feigned Opening

Rank 2

A wise leader creates the impression of weakness to disguise strength, or strength to conceal weakness.

Activation:

As a Movement and Scheme action, you may make a Performance (Air) check targeting one character in the scene who is observing you. The TN is equal to the target’s vigilance. This technique can only be used during skirmish and mass battle conflicts.

Effects:

If you succeed, the target believes you are weaker than you actually are (or stronger, if you are feigning strength), and adjusts their actions accordingly. The GM determines how the target’s behavior changes based on the situation and the nature of the feigned state.

New Opportunities:

  • Air (op)+: Choose one additional target with vigilance lower than or equal to the original target’s per (op) spent this way.
  • Air (op)+: In a mass battle, increase the TN of Attack checks targeting your cohort by 1, plus 1 per additional (op)(op) spent this way.
