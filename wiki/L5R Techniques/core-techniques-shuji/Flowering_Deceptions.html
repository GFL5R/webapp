Flowering Deceptions/h2>

Rank 1<

Activation:

Once per scene, as a Scheme and Support action, you may make a Courtesy (Air) or Tactics (Air) check targeting two other characters who are not hostile to each other. The TN of the check is equal to the highest vigilance among your targets.

Effects:

If you succeed, choose one target and increase the TN of their next check by 2. Decrease the TN of the other target's check by 2. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

New Opportunities:

  • Air (op): Neither of your targets realizes your duplicity in hindering one to help the other.
  • Air (op)(op): You can use this technique one additional time this scene.
