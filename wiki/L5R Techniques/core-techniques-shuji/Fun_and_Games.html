Fun and Games

Rank 1

Games can be fun to play or observe. They can be even more fun when one of the participants is a good sport who knows how to lose and win with grace, and how to entertain their fellow participants and the audience in the process.

Activation:

  • Water (op)+: Choose one participant in the game or an observer. Remove 1 strife per opportunity spent from that participant.
  • Water (op)(op)(op): Remove 1 strife from each participant and observer instead.
