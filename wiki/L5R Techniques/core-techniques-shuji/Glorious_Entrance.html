Glorious Entrance

Rank 2

A courtier who often travels from place to place knows the importance of making an entrance. After all, a first impression can set the tone for all future negotiations. Whether arriving in style or simply making a bold proclamation to attract attention, a courtier who knows how to draw all eyes to themselves sets the table for all forthcoming negotiations.

Activation:

When you enter a scene, you may make a Performance (Fire) check. The TN of this check is equal to the difference between the glory rank of the character in the scene with the highest glory and your glory rank, to a minimum of 1.

Effects:

If you succeed, reduce the TN of Scheme and Support action checks you make by 1. This effect persists for one round, plus one additional round for every two bonus successes.

New Opportunities:

  • (op)(op)+: When you receive a glory award, increase the amount of glory you gain by 1, plus 1 per additional (op)(op) spent this way.
  • (op)(op): You gain the Emboldened condition (see page 133).
