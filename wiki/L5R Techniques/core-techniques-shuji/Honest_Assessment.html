Honest Assessment

Rank 1

Honesty is a virtue of Bushidō, but direct honesty is often seen as rudeness among the samurai—instead, one is expected to find a polite way to imply one’s meaning to spare the feelings of the other involved.

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a TN 2 Courtesy (Earth) check to appraise the weaknesses of a character.

Effects:

If you succeed, choose one of the target’s known disadvantages. The target does not apply that disadvantage to their checks until the end of the scene.

New Opportunities:

  • Earth (op)+: Choose one additional disadvantage per (op) spent this way.
  • Earth (op)(op): Reduce the TN of the target’s next skill check using the ring the disadvantage is attached to by 2.
