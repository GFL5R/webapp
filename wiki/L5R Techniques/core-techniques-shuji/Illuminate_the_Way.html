Illuminate the Way

Rank 1

Fire pushes back the darkness, but it is knowledge that truly lights up the world. On the fringes of society, one encounters those whose wisdom is gained through firsthand experience.

Activation:

Once per session at the start of a scene, or as a Support action, you may make a TN 2 Scholar skill (Fire) check targeting one character.

Effects:

If you succeed, that character treats their vigilance as 2 lower (to a minimum of 1) and their focus as 2 higher. This effect persists until the end of the scene.

New Opportunities:

  • Fire (op)+: Choose one additional target per (op) spent this way.
  • Fire (op): This effect persists until the end of the next scene instead.
