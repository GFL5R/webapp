Incite Wrath

Rank 3

Whether a war mount is a horse, camel, or elephant, it is not just a way for a warrior to move more quickly on the battlefield but also a living weapon unto itself. A skillful rider can drive their mount to lash out with hooves, tusks, or teeth, smashing foes aside with its might.

Activation:

When you make a Scheme or Support action check while riding a mount or while you have an animal companion at range 0–1 of you, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • (op)(op)+: Your mount or animal companion immediately performs a Strike action targeting one character within range of one of its readied weapons. The damage it deals increases by 1 per (op)(op) spent this way.
