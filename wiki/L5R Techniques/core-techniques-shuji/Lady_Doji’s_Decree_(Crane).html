Lady Doji’s Decree

Rank 2 (Crane)

Propriety, grace, and eloquence were of great importance to the founder of the Crane Clan. With a word, she was said to defuse the most dangerous situations. Crane Clan courtiers follow in her footsteps, forcing bystanders to listen with a single striking comment.

Activation:

Once per game session as an action, you may make a Courtesy (Void) check targeting a number of characters up to your school rank. The TN of this check is equal to the vigilance of the character with the highest status among your targets.

Effects:

If you succeed, your targets cannot perform Attack actions targeting you. This effect persists for one round, plus additional rounds equal to your bonus successes, or until you perform an Attack action.

New Opportunities:

  • Void (op)+: Choose one additional target with status lower than your first target per (op) spent this way.
  • Void (op) (op): Your targets also cannot perform Scheme actions targeting you.
