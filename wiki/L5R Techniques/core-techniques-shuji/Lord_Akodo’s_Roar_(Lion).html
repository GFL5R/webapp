Lord Akodo’s Roar

Rank 2 (Lion)

The Kami Akodo One-Eye, who founded the Lion Clan, was the greatest tactician in the Empire. His victories rested not upon the strength of his arm, but upon his keen mind and his faith in the people he rallied behind. The true general never stands alone, for they are always bolstered by the united will of their followers. With a roar that echoes through the ranks, chilling their foes and heartening their comrades, the leader charges as Akodo himself once did.

Activation:

Once per game session as an action, you may make a TN 1 Command (Void) check targeting each hostile character within a number of range bands equal to your school rank.

Effects:

If you succeed, each target suffers the Dazed condition.

New Opportunities:

  • Void (op) (op)+: After you perform this action, one other friendly character per (op) (op) spent this way at range 0–3 may immediately perform a Strike action targeting a Dazed character.
