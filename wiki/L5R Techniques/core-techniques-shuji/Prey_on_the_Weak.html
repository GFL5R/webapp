Prey on the Weak

Rank 2

Court is not a kind place, and a harsh word is no less cruel for being spoken softly and with perfect etiquette.

Activation:

As a Scheme action, you may make a TN 1 Courtesy (Air) check targeting a Compromised character.

Effects:

If you succeed, your target must choose one of the following:

  • Immediately unmask in a manner of their choosing based on the circumstances.
  • Forfeit glory equal to your Air Ring plus your bonus successes to leave the scene.

New Opportunities:

  • Air (op): If the target chooses to unmask, you learn one of their disadvantages of their choice per (op) spent this way.
  • Air (op)(op): If the target chooses to leave the scene, you notice their destination from the direction of their depar- ture or overhear from the crowd where they will retreat.
