Rallying Cry

Rank 3

The leader roars, calling their troops to battle, and the voice of an army echoes around them as the single cry is joined by many. As one, the troops surge forward to claim victory or perish in the attempt.

Activation:

As a Support action, you may make a TN 2 Command (Fire) or Performance (Fire) check targeting any number of characters in the scene (or one cohort in a mass battle).

Effects:

If you succeed, the targets are energized and excited by your rhetoric. After Step 5: Choose Kept Dice, each target adds a kept (ring) set to an (op) (st) result. This effect persists until the end of your next turn.

New Opportunities:

  • Fire (op)+: In a skirmish, each enemy in the scene suffers 1 strife per (op) spent this way.
  • Fire (op)+: In a mass battle, the enemy army suffers 1 panic per (op) spent this way.
