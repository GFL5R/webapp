Rouse the Soul

Rank 5

Many things can cloud the soul, but words can awaken it. The courtier speaks honestly and without pretension, calling upon their allies to overcome earthly circumstances and reach a mutual goal.

Activation:

When making a Social skill (Void) or Theology (Void) check targeting one or more characters, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Void (op)+: Remove one of the following conditions from one target per (op) spent this way: Afflicted, Enraged, Exhausted, Intoxicated, or Unconscious.
