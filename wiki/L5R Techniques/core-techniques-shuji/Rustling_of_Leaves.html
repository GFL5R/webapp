Rustling of Leaves

Rank 1

If everyone has heard a scandalous story, but it has no clear source, what can it be but true? The courtier cleverly spreads stories so none see them as the source.

Activation:

When you make a Social skill (Air) check to spread a rumor, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Air (op)+: Increase the TN of any check to trace the rumor back to you by 2 per (op) spent this way.
