Sear the Wound

Rank 5

Many courtiers cannot resist the opportunity to twist the knife after delivering a stabbing remark, but the wise use this power judiciously. Though somewhat cruel, the technique of bringing a rival’s weakness to the forefront of their mind to burn away at their resolve is often effective.

Activation:

When making a Social skill (Fire) check targeting one or more characters, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Fire (op): Choose a known disadvantage of one of your targets per (op) spent this way. That disadvantage applies to all of the target’s checks until the end of the scene.
