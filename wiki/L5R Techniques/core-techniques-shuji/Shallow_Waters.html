Shallow Waters

Rank 1

WA good courtier learns how to read the desires of those around them from the subtle cues of their responses in conversation, as well as how to probe for details without revealing their intentions.

Activation:

AWhen you make a Social skill (Water) check targeting a character, you may spend (op) as follows:

  • Water (op): Learn of one material item or worldly experience the target desires.
  • Water (op)(op)+: Learn the target’s ninjō (see page 38).
