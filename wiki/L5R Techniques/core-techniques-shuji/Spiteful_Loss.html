Spiteful Loss

Rank 1

A bad sport can ruin the fun for everyone in a game, embarrassing themselves in other’s eyes while leaving the participants and audience stressed and frustrated. Of course, sometimes that’s exactly the point.

Activation:

When you make a Games (Fire) check while playing a game, you may spend opportunity in the following way:

  • Fire (op): If you fail, forfeit 1 glory. Then each participant receives strife equal to 1 plus the shortfall of the check on all participants in the game.
