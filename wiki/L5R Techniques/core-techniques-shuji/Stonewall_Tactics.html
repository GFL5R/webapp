Stonewall Tactics

Rank 1

The courtier makes themself impossible for the target to ignore, raising a pressing concern that must be addressed first or even physically planting themselves between a rival and their allies.

Activation:

When making a Social skill (Earth) or Martial skill (Earth) check targeting a character, you may spend (op) in the following way:

  • Earth (op)+: Increase the TN of any check the target makes that does not target you by 1 per (op) spent this way. This effect persists until the beginning of your next turn.
