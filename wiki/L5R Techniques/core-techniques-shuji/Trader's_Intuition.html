Trader's Intuition

Rank 2

Most Rokugani samurai do not often deal with commerce personally, instead relying on servants to buy and sell goods for them if needed. In the Unicorn Clan, the line between merchants and warrior nobility is not so clearly demarcated. Many powerful Unicorn deal with their own finances, not just balancing the books but actually trading goods at the markets of Khanbulak and in cities far beyond Rokugan.

Activation:

As a Scheme action, you may make a TN 2 Commerce (Air) check targeting an object you can observe. The TN of the check is 2, or 4 if the object has the Forbidden item quality.

Effects:

If you succeed, you assess the target, learning its monetary value and rarity.

New Opportunities:

  • (op): You know a place (which you have visited) where you could sell the object for more money than it is worth in your current location.
