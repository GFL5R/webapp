Wanderer's Resolve

Rank 1

A samurai with a lord can find resolve in the loyalty they feel, but a samurai with no lord has no easy spring from which to draw resolve. This hardship and uncertainty all their own, for if they do not act, no one else will. The lives of their friends—of those who depend on them—rest solely in their hands.

Activation:

Once per scene as a Support action, if you are in a Void stance , you may focus on protecting what is important to you.

Effects:

You remove 3 fatigue and 3 strife, plus 3 fatigue and 3 strife for each Compromised or Incapacitated character in the scene with whom you share a bond (see Bonds, on page 187).
