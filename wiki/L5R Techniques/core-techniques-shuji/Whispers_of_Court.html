Whispers of Court

Rank 1

Rumors are the currency of court, and when such news is in short supply, many courtiers simply spin it themselves. They use cunning schemes and plausible half-truths to bait their webs of intrigue.

Activation:

As a Scheme action, you may make a Courtesy (Air) check targeting each character in the scene. The TN of this check is equal to the lowest vigilance among your targets.

Effects:

If you succeed, you create a rumor that sweeps across the room like a storm. Everyone in the scene hears it, and characters with vigilance lower than your bonus successes even come to give it some credence. At the GM’s discretion, a character can transmit a shūji technique over time and distance via epistolary or similar means, though it generally only affects a single intended recipient.

New Opportunities:

  • Air (op): Learn one other rumor that is spreading in the current scene.
