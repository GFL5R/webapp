Your Enemy’s Arrows

Rank 4

A skilled commander sees the enemy’s troops as if they were under their command, then issues orders to their own army such that the enemy will fall into place for their destruction. By erasing the distinction between the foe’s assets and one’s own in their mind, a general can see the battlefield as a whole, and maneuver the pieces to the endgame of their choosing.

Activation:

Once per scene as a Scheme action targeting another character who can perceive you or your cohort, you may make a Tactics (Water) check. The TN is equal to the target’s focus. You may stake up to 3 glory on your success to reduce the TN by that amount.

Effects:

If you succeed, the next time your target accrues momentum points toward an objective, add those momentum points to your objective instead.

If you fail, you lose the glory you staked upon your success.

New Opportunities:

  • Water (op): Your target remains unaware of your machinations. If you failed, regain your staked glory.
  • Water (op)+: Gain 1 momentum point toward your current goal.
  • Water (op)+(op): All characters in the scene with vigilance lower than or equal to (op) spent this way are unaware of your machinations.
