# GFL2 Loading Screens

This is a list of lore bits shared during loading screens in Girls' Frontline 2: Exilium.

  * Cities in White Zones under the jurisdiction of the URNC government are connected by a network of long-distance, high-speed special transportation known as the "Intercontinental Railway", which facilitates the movement of people and goods between cities.
  * Building on the existing fixed purification tower technology, the NSU and URNC governments jointly developed a new mobile purification tower.
  * On January 17, 2030, the Beilan Island Incident occurred, marking the first widespread release of Collapse radiation from the remains of an unknown ancient civilization.
  * In the 20-plus years since the Beilan Island Incident, accidents in North America, Western Europe, and Central Europe have caused Collapse radiation to spread to most parts of the world.
  * In 2065, G&K reached an agreement with the NSU government to break up the organization, leading to the dissolution of G&K Private Military Company and Security Consultant Company. That same year, the Non-Military Forces Administration was established.
  * In 2065, restrictions on the size of PMCs led to a surge in bounty hunters. By 2067, B.R.I.E.F. had emerged as the top governing body for bounty hunters.
  * According to our records, the group known as the "Varjagers" appeared around 2066.
  * In 2066, the URNC government started drawing up reconstruction policies focused on purifying contamination zones and expanding urban development.
  * In 2066, the NSU government signed an agreement with the URNC government, beginning the process of merging into the URNC.
  * In 2067, the URNC government started a project named "Lazarus" to clean up and restore contamination zones.
  * In 2067, the Union of Rossartrist Nations Coalition and the Neo-Soviet Union signed the "European Collapse Radiation Contamination Zone Restoration and Revitalization Joint Development Agreement".
  * In 2067, the NSU government started rolling out several five-year "revitalization" plans, collectively known as the "New Westward Movement".
  * In 2067, the URNC started tightening the approval process for new T-Doll patent licenses.
  * In 2067, the Sangvis Ferri Rebellion was completely quelled, and Sangvis Ferri's product technology patents automatically entered the public domain.
  * In 2067, the Statesec-led operation to ban Doll arenas was launched successfully.
  * By 2070, most of the safety zones in South America, Europe, and northern Asia were integrated into the URNC.
  * Since 2070, the percentage of wild animals suffering from E.L.I.D. and displaying severe mutations has increased yearly. Due to their unique appearance, these creatures are often referred to as "ELID".
  * In 2071, 16Lab launched the third generation of Doll technology.
  * The URNC Contamination Zone Environmental Analysis Team first observed the appearance of fusion-type ELIDs around 2071. This type of creatures were recorded as "Boojum".
