# Girard Group

The **Girard Group** (吉拉尔集团), member of the Orlog Conglomerate, is a powerful industrial group created and based in Odesa, and led by Rurik Girard (留里克·吉拉尔). It is involved in the production and research of a variety of products, spanning consumer to military market, and has also established charitable foundations. The company is involved in the Purification Project and Rurik is a member of the Parliament of Odesa Oblast, also granting them political weight. Girard also produces Third-Generation Dolls, such as Darture and Sextans, who was considered their finest product. Their Dolls are however considered vastly inferior to the Third-Gen Dolls produced by Persica, and they might be involved in the theft of Persica's work on new-gen Dolls and Remolding Pattern. Darture and Sextans were involved in Girard's mysterious “Horoscope Program”.

The group enjoys both a favorable reputation in Odesa and exerts a considerable influence over the city, but it aims to expand its influence to other safe zones in Eastern Europe. Moreover, after they completed the purification of the region, Girard's jurisdiction over Odesa expired but Statesec suspected that Girard's corporate armed forces would attempt to take control of the city, so the URNC's Non-Military Forces Administration were sent to investigate the group.

In 2074, the Girard Group issued TASK-3115, a B.R.I.E.F. bounty hunting contract consisting of delivering the box secretly containing Helena to Darren Claude in Odesa. After the incident in ODE-01, the group covered Claude after his failed attempt to recover Helena from the Elmo bounty hunters left a trail of destruction in the satellite city. An ongoing mystery is the nature and extent of the relationship between Girard and Helena, since the group appears to lack the biotechnical capabilities needed to have created her.
