# Griffin & Kryuger: Life at the base

## Life at the base

The Commander's base at Area S09 is particularly relaxed and gives a lot of individual freedom to its Dolls, while others enforce human-only areas and have the Dolls remain in their maintenance pods when not in combat.

Most G&K Dolls are procured through IOP, but a few of them are bought from other sources. Regardless of how they joined G&K, they receive a salary and holidays like human employees. Most of them spend it on their hobbies, in activities in the towns outside the base, or on food and drinks at Springfield's cafe. G&K provides dorms for the employees and pays for the basic expenses of life at the base including ammo and cafeteria food as long as they fit a certain quota. Depending on the branch they are working for, Dolls may select assignments they want to participate in on a first-come-first-served basis and follow no set schedule, or be selected for specific assignments. Unless they are provably related to a work injury, repair costs are deducted from the Doll's salary.

While all Dolls can potentially participate in direct combat, not all of them are regularly deployed. The Base Overview assigns Dolls to the Frontline Combat Team and Response Combat Team, but also to the Border Surveillance Corps, Integrated Logistic Department and T-Doll Club. Some act as a reserve force while others patrol the region or help run the base, and even the ceremonial guards at Griffin HQ can be Dolls. Dolls can request to change job. Aside from their day job, some Dolls also have side gigs, like HG GSh-18 and RF Type 4 who are published manga artists.

Grifchan is an anonymous forum website for G&K employees moderated by G&K command, where they can discuss and share gossips. AR MDR is an avid user of it. G&K also often organize events for employees to relax and deepen their friendship.

A G&K Doll can receive a Neural Upgrade either by being selected by the Commander as a reward for good performances and the will to grow past their limits, to solve a dangerous issue with their body or neural cloud, by submitting an application to be approved or by paying for the upgrade with their own money.
