# Griffin & Kryuger: Members until 2064

## Members until 2064

Main article: List of Griffin & Kryuger squads

For the assignment of each Doll at the Area S09 base, read Organization Overview.

  * Berezovich Kryuger (co-founder and CEO)
  * Griffin Lyons (co-founder)
  * Helianthus (second-in-command)
  * The Commander (Area S09 regional tactical commander)
  * Kalina (Area S09 logistics officer)
  * Angelia (tactical commander, temporarily)
  * SMG UMP45's former commander
  * SMG Cx4 Storm's former commander
  * AR HK416's former commander
  * Commander Novikov (might be fictional)
  * Intern commander who participated in Persica's Maze Cage experiment
  * Various Tactical Dolls (mercenaries)
  * Various Technical Fairies (UAVs)

Griffin & Kryuger also employs human personnel for various tasks such as Doll maintenance. They are seldom mentioned in the game but can be seen in Girls' Frontline: Doll's Song.
