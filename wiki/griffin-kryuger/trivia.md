# Griffin & Kryuger: Trivia

## Trivia

  * The griffin is often used as a symbol of nobility, courage and military might. Meanwhile, “Kryuger” is a variation of the German surname “Krüger”, meaning a tavern-keeper or potter. The two parts of the company's name are opposed in status, much like the roles of its two founders.
    * In the original Girls' Frontline website, “Kryuger” was mistakenly interpreted to mean “weaver” instead.
  * The Griffin helicopter seen in Girls' Frontline 2: Exilium resembles a Sikorsky HH-60 Pave Hawk, also seen when deploying echelons in Girls' Frontline, albeit equipped with four missile pods like an AH-60L Battle Hawk. A typical Tactical Dolls combat insertion is done from 10 meters above ground, their bodies capable of withstanding an unassisted descent.
    * The number “794” appearing on the loading screen of Girls' Frontline is the same number found on the evacuation helicopter appearing at the end of _Metal Gear Solid V: Ground Zeroes_.
