# Mangi Security Service

**Mangi Security Service** (曼尼安保) is a PMC active in Eastern Europe in 2074 and an antagonist faction in Girls' Frontline 2: Exilium.

Mangi was originally the security group of Cecht FABN, but was spun off into a PMC, probably for fiscal reasons. Despite only being registered with NOMFA as a small PMC with under 100 employees, Mangi employs around 2000 well-equipped human mercenaries, including an elite force called the “Omen” (噩兆, also translated “Mishaps”). The Omen earned its name within the company due to them being often called to clean up after Paradeus, which implies fighting Boojums and other monstrosities. They are equipped in consequence with heavy weapons and IFVs.

In 2071, Mangi collaborated with the Girard Group on the mysterious Project Coronal Hole, recruiting the former Griffin & Kryuger T-Doll Nikketa as a research subject for third-generation Dolls. This allowed Nikketa to become an insider agent for the Public Security Administration, and she was still acting as a mole in 2074. Adjutant Lentine led the Omen for a cleanup operation in an underground Paradeus lab some time after April 2074 and suffered heavy casualties against the Commander and their Dolls. Lentine led a punishing attack on the Elmo soon after, but were still pushed back.

## Trivia

  * The Mangi logo first appeared in July 2021 on one of Pandea work's character sheets. It appeared again the same year on Dushevnaya's artwork for Project Neural Cloud. The logo made an easily-missed appearance in the May 2022 trailer for Girls' Frontline 2: Exilium, a game where Pandea work is credited as Lead Concept Artist. It then appeared again in March 2023 on SG DP-12's Neural Upgrade artwork. The faction was properly introduced in the universe with Aphelion in 2024.
