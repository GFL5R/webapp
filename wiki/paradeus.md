# Paradeus

**Paradeus** (帕拉蒂斯) is a violent non-state actor formed in Germany, described as a terrorist group and a cult. It acts as the third and main antagonistic faction of Girls' Frontline, introduced in Continuum Turbulence. Before its true name was revealed in Isomer, it was only known as _The White Faction_ due to their primary color. Possessing advanced technology, it recruits Yellow Zone populations by promising them a cure for ELID. Paradeus' motto is the Rossartrist slogan “The shining beacon in a brave new world”, the same as Griffin & Kryuger. Their troops are led by the Nytos, who are clones blurring even further the line between human and machine who can be used in battle, as command units, or even as body doubles to infiltrate the upper society.

In the 2050s and 2060s, Paradeus was controlled by the Oberstein family, Rudolf von Oberstein and “William” von Oberstein, both referred to as “Father” (or occasionally "Master"), who pursued control of Relic Technology. Aside from forming fanatic communities and militias in Yellow Zones, Paradeus uses a technology known as "Technical Theory of Biological Human Automation" to create more-than-human soldiers and operated a powerful surveillance apparatus called the Bee Sting System. After the Battle of the Dead Sea and the Battle of Frankfurt of 2064, Paradeus has significantly scaled back its operations but continues its sinister plans in the Red and Yellow Zones in Eastern Europe.

In Girls' Frontline, Paradeus units use a force field applying a percentage damage reduction to incoming attacks, which can be destroyed by Heavy Ordnance Corps.

## History

### Formation

The precursor of Paradeus is the European Yellow Zone cult called Evangel Portal, based in the Rhine region. Founded in the 2030s by a man named William, the cult preached that a Holy Spirit would descend on Earth and take the faithful through the "Evangel Portal" (sometimes translated "Gate of Evangel") to reach the Sacred Land, free from mortal suffering and Collapse radiation. During World War Three, Hanna von Oberstein and her daughter Lunasia joined the cult, becoming its Holy Mother and Holy Maiden. Evangel Portal was already an influential group due to its focus on accumulating the wealth surrendered by its adherents rather than violent action, and the purported miracles of its saints increased their influence further. All the purported "miracles" performed by the cult were in fact merely mental manipulation and countless people were killed in hope for a miraculous end to the World War Three. Hanna eventually died from ritual suicide, cutting her throat along with many other cultists to reach the Promised Land. Evangel Portal was violently disbanded by the German government during the First Purge against violent Yellow Zone activities.

After Hanna's death, Lunasia returned into the custody of her father Rudolf von Oberstein, but he submitted his daughter to more atrocities in order to turn her into a Relics key. Rudolf intended to use Relics to protect Germany. Codenamed Project Valkyrie, these experiments were led by a man also named William (it's unclear if he was the same who founded Evangel Portal) at Evangel Portal's successor: Paradeus. Evangel Portal and Project Valkyrie gave Lunasia a mad hatred for mankind, and she decided to accept her transformation into a key in order to use the Relics to destroy humanity. Lunasia used her cult influence to contact Martha Meitner and tasked her to complete her transformation, using Lunasia's own followers for research. A former member of the Holy Grail Project at the Relics Agency, Meitner herself had become obsessed with Relic Technology in order to find a way to resurrect her daughter Sana, and joined Paradeus under the name Laplace. Lunasia specified that her mother be "the first sacrifice", and asked her brother to turn her corpse into something different. She also made him promise to also resurrect her if she was killed and ensure she would carry out the destruction of the world.

Lunasia eventually died during Project Valkyrie in 2049. Laplace had each organ preserved separately, and her brain cut into slices for later study. When Lunasia's young brother and Rudolf's son Ballard von Oberstein discovered the fate of his sister, he stole brain slices and fled from Paradeus before being sheltered by Nita(妮塔), a woman with Collapse radiation resistance who lived alone in the woods near Frankfurt. Following the suggestion of a hallucination of his sister, Ballard killed Nita and changed her into a bio-weapon under his control using a portable surgical lab. By creating the first Nyto under duress, Ballard proved to his father that he was capable of holding a position in Paradeus despite his age, further arguing that only the Oberstein family and not Laplace should lead the organization. In truth, Ballard needed to gather the power necessary to kill Rudolf and avenge his sister. From then on, the position of “Father” in Paradeus was concurrently held by Rudolf and Ballard, and Ballard took the title of William, which Laplace didn't want to use despite her position as Paradeus' head scientist.

The earliest activity of Paradeus known to the Stasi took place in 2060, in the European Yellow Zone, in Southern Germany, where an illegal orphanage was found to house multiple young Nyto clones. Before that, Paradeus had recruited many brilliant minds in diverse fields of research from around the world into the front company Galatea Corporation. At some point, Paradeus established a lab in the depths of the abandoned Paldiski Submarine Base near Tallinn, Estonia, on the site of a Starfish construct. They had a long partnership with the Vory v Zakone.

Shortly after Paradeus' existence became known, the Balkan Section of the Stasi's Foreign Intelligence Service launched "Operation Cornerstone of Peace" to counter it, placing Major Kain Schwaben as operation commander. The Neo-Soviet Statesec quickly approached Schwaben to collaborate on the matter. Soviet intelligence enabled the Stasi to identify one of Paradeus' collaborators spotted in Belgrade, Captain Yegor, leading to a clique led by General Carter and an unknown group based near Sarajevo.

### Operations in Eastern Europe

As allies of General Carter, Paradeus intervened as a fourth party during the Great Retreat in 2064, after the KCCO turned against Griffin and Kryuger. Their original goal was to capture Elisa after she melded with AR M4A1, but this plan failed. The Nytos led the Paradeus troops in an effort to wipe out the remaining G&K troops, but they failed when the commanding Nyto Black was incapacitated by AR M4 SOPMOD II and self-destructed. A separate force led by a Nyto White successfully captured the Commander before intending to sacrifice herself in an assault on the G&K forces coming to retrieve the Commander, claiming she would reach awakening. The Paradeus forces were pinned down when SMG RO635 used her megaphone to lure ELID-infected toward them, but the Nyto White refused to attack them, calling them “the Baptized” and opening herself to a headshot from RF M82A1.

Paradeus treated the Commander's radiation poisoning during nine weeks before interrogating them to determine the whereabouts of Squad 404, Angelia and Elisa. Squad 404 and G&K raided the base and rescued the Commander, but they encountered Paradeus again three months later while searching for an informant possessing information about Angelia in Belgrade.

During the Belgrade international cooperation talks, Paradeus attempted to kidnap Gilda Ulrich and exchange her to Statesec for confidential information and to recover a lost Pike node. During the Paldiski Incident, the Commander, K and Angelia discovered that Paradeus had been using the lost city of Tallinn as an ELID experiment site for Isomers and lost refugees, and they found more information about them at the Paldiski Submarine Base where they had established and recently abandoned a Nyto lab.

### Operations in Germany

Throughout the years, the government of Germany had become entangled with Paradeus under Rudolf's influence. Paradeus notably developed the intelligence system that was used by the Stasi, the Bee Sting system, control of which was split in two administrator keys resting with each organization. When Romy Riefenstahl became director of the Stasi in 2064, she started a covert campaign to bring Bee Sting under the Stasi's complete control.

In September 2064, Angelia tracked down William in Bremen and uncovered the network of Paradeus allies such as the Flora Institute providing them with modified Epiphyllum flowers and the Berle Orphanage involved in their child trafficking. Angelia also discovered that some Nytos like Morridow were infiltrated among normal humans when she attempted to abduct her. In Berlin, Angelia and the Commander worked to foil Paradeus' Operation Cocoon by intercepting the Iaso's Boxes they planned to spread Collapse Radiation all over the city, in order to locate ELID immunosomes. Angelia visited William's old home, but she was betrayed and captured by her own team member who wanted to become a Nyto. Paradeus also manipulated their rogue agent Machlian to lure the OGAS consciousness called Dandelion and the Commander into a trap, but they survived with the help of M4A1(?) and captured Morridow. They however lost her while attempting to exchange her for Angelia, and the Nyto revealed to William that M4A1 was still of interest to Paradeus.

As Paradeus shut down its operations in Berlin, they blackmailed the German government by organizing the massacre of a rogue Stasi team in one of the Ministry of Defense's plants, threatening to disclose the incident and destabilize the government if they revealed their ties with Paradeus. The Stasi struck back by weeding out “Polar Bear”, a Paradeus agent infiltrated in Statesec, and with the help of the Stasi's director Romy Riefenstahl, the Commander organized Operation Aeneas to locate and infiltrate Paradeus' headquarters in Avernus, in the Dead Sea Black Zone near Berlin. By that point, the Commander had become known as the only person to have not only opposed and survived, but successfully cornered Paradeus.

By the end of the Battle of the Dead Sea, Paradeus had lost their main base in Germany as well as several high-ranking Nytos against the combined assault of Griffin & Kryuger and Statesec.

### Downfall

Paradeus has been considered wiped out during the Battle of Frankfurt. The organization fell due to both external and internal factors: the Stasi aimed to rid the German government of Paradeus' influence and supported the efforts of the Commander, a Neo-Soviet mercenary from Griffin & Kryuger who had vowed to destroy Paradeus and kill William. William himself had ensured that Paradeus' HQ in Avernus would fall and arranged the assassinations of Laplace and Rudolf, ensuring his full control over Paradeus before joining the URNC and putting himself in the service and under the protection of the Prometheans to further his own plans, but he was eventually killed by the Commander.

In a 2071 article on Paradeus, it was described as the most dangerous cult of the 21st century, rampaging through the contaminated zones of Central and Eastern Europe unopposed by both bandits and governments. But the general public was not aware of the circumstances of its disappearance in the mid-2060s, so sudden that scavengers who ventured in their former strongholds found a wealth of their resources left behind. The most widespread narrative, especially in Neo-Soviet military circles, told that Paradeus was formed by former NATO soldiers trying to take down the NSU, and that they were destroyed during an all-out offensive. Claimed evidence supporting this story include the involvement of both the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the Griffin & Kryuger PMC led by former Internal Affairs member Berezovich Kryuger, and the suspicious movements of the Western Army Group, disturbances at the border and mass arrests in the Neo-Soviet military in 2064.

Theories considered fringe included claims that Paradeus were technologically advanced aliens colonizers that left Earth after finding it too contaminated, or that Paradeus was built by a scientist seeking revenge and was dealt a killing blow to its backbone by a single member of Griffin & Kryuger. The NSU never acknowledged the existence of Paradeus.

### Remnants

Paradeus survived through local cults worshiping Collapse radiation at the border between Yellow and Red Zones in Eastern Europe. They researched the best ways to gather new converts, which led to the creation of Nemesis and the Eutis "miracle drink", impeded the Purification Project and were one of the rare targets that the Varjagers wouldn't attack. It's unclear whether Paradeus took over already existing cults or if they created the cults themselves.

The Elmo discovered contamination zone sects related to Paradeus in April 2074. Led by Niters, these sects promised to cure ELID sufferers in exchange for their service as Unitates servants, while a Black Nyto acted as a higher commander. Their goal was to find the “Lost Cavitas” on behalf of a certain Lady Immore. Another branch studied the potential of fusing Collapse-infused plant matter with animals and humans. One of their programs, the "Powehi Plan", involved artificially growing large Collapse crystals to raise on demand the density of Collapse radiation in certain areas. The links between Paradeus and the Girard Group, as well as the biopharmaceutical company suspected to have produced the Helena clones, are still unclear. Blusphere and Mangi Security Service were deployed to secure areas where Paradeus' research was discovered by third parties to prevent information leaks.

The Commander noted the sharp decline in the technological capability and manpower of these sects as a sign that William was no longer affiliated with them.

### Nytos

“  | We need hearts filled with wisdom, courage, loyalty and sacrifice. The only thing that is unnecessary is rationality.—  | ”
---|---|---

Nytos (涅托, sometimes called “Baptized”) are girls or women (there are no known male Nytos) whose consciousness has been physically embodied and functions like a Neural Cloud. This enables them to interface with digital networks like Tactical Dolls or establish a direct connection with other Nytos, but also exposes them to hacking. This biological neural cloud has been dubbed Virtual Cognition Image (VCI) or "virtual consciousness". The people turned into Nytos are mostly mass-produced clones, but also acquired through human trafficking and kidnapping of survivors of a Iaso's Box. They are also said to be created from corpses. Nimogen was said to be part of an “experimental series”.

The basic Nyto Black and Nyto White are mass-produced, but among the named Nytos only Tareus was known to have multiple active clones at once. Most Nytos have an appearance close to AR M4A1 and have their bodies modified with cybernetic enhancements, but they still bleed like normal humans. Others remain mostly unmodified save for their brain, making them closer to humans, depending on the manner Paradeus intend to use them. Cybernetic enhancements typically include replacement of the legs and arms and added head equipment for lower-ranked Nytos, while higher-ranked Nytos can receive less visible enhancements and attachable extra limbs for combat.

Nytos are all byproducts of William's attempts to create a pan-immunosome but none of them have been a successful experiment. Exceptions to this creation process are the proto-Nytos Sana and Nemhran, who is an “abnormal” Nyto possessing emotions without desires. Because all known Nytos are considered imperfect, they are all technically Isomers, though they are rarely properly referred as such. According to Morridow, individual Nytos cannot be recreated once dead, as Tactical Dolls can. Around 11,000 Nytos were created during Project Cocoon alone. Nytos are produced in Avernus, where it was estimated that 99% of the specimens that undergo combat training die in the process.

The hierarchy of Nytos is based on their closeness to William's ideal Nyto, and higher Nytos often despise lower ones. Higher-ranked Nytos tend to have white hair. Nyto Whites are considered to have a greater sense of self than Nyto Blacks and have complete authority over them, though they still share the same gestalt mind. Gray and Narciss had authority over Nyto Whites, while Morridow had authority over Gray and Narciss. Lower-ranked Nytos usually call higher-ranked ones “Big Sister”, though some with significant bonds use other titles like “Teacher” or no title at all. Higher-ranked Nytos don't emit the same characteristic signal as lower-ranked ones and refuse to be called Nytos, though there are no alternative name for them yet apart from descriptions such as “augmented human”. The strict command hierarchy means Paradeus units not commanded by a Nyto will stop moving. Nytos appear to have a built-in failsafe that can be activated by William: he once induced instantaneous brain death in a Nyto White by snapping his fingers.

It is possible for a Nyto to weaken or break free of Paradeus' indoctrination, such as with Adeline, Alina and Tareus.

### Niters

By 2074, the remnants of Paradeus had developed a lower-grade version of the Nytos called the Niters. Niters retain their mechanical body parts and virtual cognition image, but unlike Nytos, they rely on nanomachines for mental control and their combat abilities are closer to those of a Tactical Doll. Niters acted as local leaders for Yellow Zone cults in Eastern Europe.

### Isomers

While Dandelion calls all Nytos "Isomers" as she sees them as imperfect copies, Persica uses this term for "failed" Nytos candidates who didn't live up to expectations and were abandoned by Paradeus. In chemistry, isomers are molecules with the same number and types of atoms, but organized differently.

Isomers aim to merge with a similar but perfected consciousness like their own to be liberated.

### Unitates

The regular soldiers of the Paradeus remnant sects of 2074 are called _Unitates_ (singular _Unitas_). They are usually ELID sufferers from the contamination zones who have been promised recovery in exchange for their devotion.
